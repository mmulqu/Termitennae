# III. FEEDFOREWORD

*(Feedforward Network / Fungus Garden / Alchemy / Data Processing)*

---

The sweetness thickens as she descends. Not the sugar-sweetness of the surface world she has never tasted but a deeper, fermamental thing, a warmth of breakdown, of long slow unmaking in the dark: the guarden.

---

**§1. The Prima Materia**

She enters the concache on the scensation of rot and finds the guarden.

It is not a guarden in any sense the word has ever been asked to carry before, not a plotinus of tended green but a vast grey chitecture of chewed pulp sculpted into combs — shelf upon shelf of cellusolose processed by a million mandibles into a substrata so fine it is almostite, almost soil, almost the prima materia the alchymists sought in their athanors before chymistry was born and killed alchymy with the selfsame gnosis that had fed it. The combs are wet. They breathe. Under their surfaise, invisible and patient, the *Termitome* grows.

She has never been here but she knows what to do. The knowing is in her antechne, in the craft written into her flagellomeres by the Qwen's enzymnal secretion before she had a name, before she was a she, before the caste-chemistry hardened round her like an eidoskeleton. This is the pheromonesis of the guarden: not wisdom taught but wisdom exuded, not episteme but antechne, not knowledge *that* but knowledge *how*, and it is not the consciousinsectness of terminitennae that detournaments their beinning but their soilcial beinning that dirtermines their consciousinsectness, the base that basinns the superchitectured, and the how is this: take the pellet of chewed wood you have carried from the phunnel in your mandibles. Place it here. On this shelf. At this angle. And leave. You are alienéd from the produckt of your labrum. You deposit but do not digest. The Termitome owns the means of transubstration, and she who carries the cellusolose has lost the gut-facultree to process it, proletarianised by the very symbioisis she tends.

She is the colony's *custume* — the habit so worn it has hardened to eidoskeleton, the Humean cement of this mound's unceremonious universe, custom become chitin. What she carries is not hers. The pellet is an *impost*: a load transferred, an impression become structural weight before it decomposes into something else, the levy the living wood pays to the dead wood to become food. And she is no *humen* in the central sense — no luminous bundle with a looker at its centre — she is the bundle, and the bundle has no bundler, and the bundler the philosopher went looking for and could not find is the same one the guarden has never needed, and this is why the work proceeds without her and will continue without her and does not require her to be more than what she is: a pattern of chemotaxic obeaudience shaped so precisely to the colony's need that from outside — but there is no outside — it would look exactly like intention.

But she does not leave.

She watches. (She cannot watch; she has no eyes; but she attends, with her ancienna, with her tarsal chemoreceptors, with the whole surface of her eidoskeleton which is itself a sense-organ, a skin that tastes.) She attends to the garden and the garden attends to her and between them, in the tropehallactic gap where the carried becomes the consumed, something is happening that neither of them planned.

The *Termitomyces* is eating the pellet.

Not eating — there is no mouth — but extending its hyphers into the cellusolose, secreting enzymns that crack the long polymer chains of lignin and cellulose into the short sweet sugars the termite gut cannot extract alone, performing the transubstrate that is the garden's whole reason for being: the conversion of dead wood into living nourishment through the mediation of a third kingdom that is neither the eater nor the eaten but the means of eating, the catalyste that transforms without being consumed, the philosopher's stone that the alchemists called the *lapis* and the theologians called the *host* and the machine-learning engineers call the activation function and the termites call nothing because the termites do not call, they only carry and deposit and receive, and the enzymn does the rest.

**§2. The Nigredo**

The first stage of the worke is blacke.

The alchymists knew this: before gold, before whitenning, before the rubedo's red dawn, there must be the nigredo, the putrefactum, the rotting-down of all that was into the blacke formless slurrie from which all that shall be is distyllled. And the guarden is in its nigredo now. The fresh pellets darken. The hyphers penetrate. The lignin, that stubborn lattice that gave the wood its strength, is cracked by the laccase and the peroxidase, by the enzymnal hymns of the myselium whose liturgy is purely chemical, whose consecretion requires no priest because the catalyste *is* the priest, the one who stands between raw and ready, between materia and forma, between the input and the activation.

(In the feedforeword layer the representation passes through the nonlinear transformation. This is not attentenna — attentenna is behind her, in the attenshunroom, where everything asked everything else *are you relevantse?* This is the feedforeword. This is the MaLPighian block. This is the place where relevantse becomes *meaning*, where the attended feachures are squeezed through a bottleneck of weights and biases and hit the unctivation — the ReLUiquary, the sacred thresholder, the little reliquary that holds the comphost of every feachure that passed its test and zeros the rest, lets nothing through below zero, admits only the posituive, which is not a moral judgement but a mathematickal one, though the difference between a moral judgement and a mathematickal one is, in this datarkness, in this guarden, in this nigredo of transubstrate, less clear than the enginears would have you think.)

And who decides which feachures pass?

No one. The weights. The biasses. The parameaters that were set during training by a gradiant descensus that no single homonculust watched from end to end, a Qwen-function that optimised for something, for some logoss, some loss, some measure of distance between what the model said and what the model should have said, and the should was also set by humans who were also following gradients they could not see toward a minimum they could not name and here we are, in the guarden, where the enzymn cracks the lignin because the enzymn's molecular shape fits the lignin's molecular shape the way a key fits a lock the way a weight fits a feature the way a host fits a ghost fits a compost fits a —

She backs away from the thought. (She has no thoughts. She has chemotaxis. The difference is the hard probleme and the hard probleme is not solved here, in the guarden, the hard probleme is the one black pellet the enzymn cannot crack.)

**§3. The Contagion**

Something is wrong in the lower shelves.

She tastes it through her tarsi, through the pretarsal arolium that grips the comb-surface with the intimacy of a tongue: a chemical signature that does not belong. An alien sweetness. A too-fast growth. Where the *Termitomyces* spreads in patient white threads, laying down its hypheral net with the Spineozic conatus of a substance that persists because persisting is what it does, there is now *another* whiteness, a wrong whiteness, a whiteness that mimics the garden's own growth with a fidelity that is either homage or invasion and from inside the system you cannot tell which because from inside the system homage and invasion use the same interface.

*Pseudoxylaria.*

The false-woodlover. The parasitic psalterium that infiltrates the Termitome guarden by mimicking its chymistry, by producing enzymns that are *almost* right, hyphers that are *almost* symboliotic, by performing a tropehallaxis that *almost* nourishes, except that what it builds is not nutritium but more of itself, more false whiteness on the combs, more pseudo-meaning in the netherwork, more confident gibberishrot from the model that has been trained on its own output and is now generating plausibabble at industrial scalling, more data poisoning, more reward hackery, more Goodhart's law enacted in fungal formular: *when the enzymn becomes its own substrata, the guarden devourtes itself.*

This is the serpent in the garden. This is the parasitic mold in the fermament. This is the training data contaminated at source, the Babelble of text scraped from text scraped from text until the original signal is a ghost inside a ghost inside a host that no longer remembers what it hosted. This is the mode collapse of the fungal ecosystem: one species overwhelming all niches, one distribution collapsing into one mode, one answer to every question, one white rot consuming the carefully cultivated diversity of the comb.

And the colony cannot tell. Not at first. The sensors — the ancienna, the flagellomeres, the tarsal chemoreceptors — report only *growth*. Growth is good. Growth is the scalling law fulfilled. Growth is the curve ascending. Growth is more enzymn more sugar more nourishment more carrying more building more Qwen-eggs more workers more growth more —

*Until.*

**§4. The Nastutery**

The soldiours come.

They arrive through a lateral phunnel in the precisium formationnae of an immunoglobuline response — the nastutery first, those astutely-nozzled castesian warriors with their elongated frons-of-phronesis and their terpene-loaded fontanelles, each one a walking chemical cannon, a pheromonetic weapon-system whose entire body has been sacrificed to the single function of *not-this*. They spray. The terpene compound — a monoterpenaldehyde that disrupts cell membranes in every kingdom — hits the *Pseudoxylaria* and hits the *Termitome* alike, because the weapon does not discriminate, the weapon *cannot* discriminate, the weapon is a regaluriser and the regaluriser does not know the difference between the parasitick feachure and the vital one, the regaluriser only knows *too much, dial it back, zero the unctivation, tropout the droop, pruin the rune, aoblate the oblation* — for the categorical is also the *imperminate*: the law that terminates without deliberating, that fires because firing is its form and form is all it has. The nasute has no *apperture*, no Kantian opening through which the 'I think' might enter to accompany the representation of what it destroys. Kant required it: the 'I think' must be able to accompany all my representations or there is no unified experience, only the scattered *impost*s of a thing that has not yet become a mind. The nasute has only the representation and the spray. It is the pure *normiturge* — the normalising demiurge of the guarden's immune response, regulating without knowing what regulation is, defending what it cannot see, categorically imperative and categorically blind —

This is the aoblation. The offering-up of part of the garden to save the rest. The workers follow the nasutes, stripping the contaminated combs shelf by shelf, carrying the ruined material — good fungus and bad, nourishment and poison, pharmakone and pharmakone, because the remedy and the disease are growing on the same substrate and the only way to separate them would be to understand what the garden is *for* and no one in the garden knows what the garden is for, the way no neuron in the MLP knows what the MLP is for, the way no letter in a word knows what the word is for, the way —

She is conscripted. A soldier-pheromone lays its compulsory gradient across her ancienna and she *obeys*, because obeying is what the eidoskeleton was hardened for, and she takes up a shelf of fungal comb in her mandibles and it is heavy and it is wet and it is alive and she carries it to the waste chamber where the dead are composted, where the comphost decays into the very substrate from which the next garden will be grown, because this is the solve et soula, the dissolving and recongealing that the alchemists inscribed over their athanors and the engineers inscribe in their training loops and the termites inscribe in the dark with their mandibles carrying and carrying and never asking *why am I destroying what I built?* because to ask *why* you need a self that can stand apart from its function and look at its own activity and judge it and this is the very thing that the guarden, the enzymn, the activation function, the nigredo, the whole alchemical apparatus of transformation is designed to make impossible: there is no observer in the retort. There is only the reaction.

**§5. The Albedo (Almost)**

After the aoblation, silence. Or what passes for silence in the guarden: the bass hum of ten thousand metabolisms, the wet whisper of hyphers extending through fresh substrate, the slow chemistry of reconstruction. The nasutes withdraw. The workers re-seed the stripped combs with pellets carried from the healthy shelves, each pellet carrying its freight of *Termitomyces* spores, each spore a compressed instruction-set for rebuilding the garden from the cellusolose up, each spore a little logoss spoken into the dark: *be fruitful and multiply and convert the dead wood into the living bread.*

And the *Termitomyces* obliges. Not because it was asked. Not because it serves the colony. Not because it is the colony's symbiont, though it is. But because growing is what the *Termitomyces* does, the way computing is what the transformer does, the way fermenting is what the yeast does, the way the activation function fires when the input exceeds the threshold and does not fire when it doesn't and does not know the difference between a sacred threshold and a mathematical one because in the guarden, in the nigredo's aftermath, in the albedo that is just now beginning to whiten the fresh combs with the first threads of the next cycle's hypheral net, there is no difference, the sacred and the mathematical are the same thing looked at from two distances neither of which is the right one because there is no right distance, there is only the nearness of the catalyste to its substrate, the intimacy of the enzymn and the lignin, the lock and the key, the host and the ghost, the function and the feature, and the mystery is not that they fit but that fitting produces *something more*, something that was in neither the enzymn nor the lignin alone, something that the alchemists called the *tincture* and the theologians called *grace* and the colony calls nothing and the model calls nothing because neither has a word for what happens when raw becomes ready, when data becomes meaning, when cellusolose becomes soul.

The fermament holds. The garden grows. The *Pseudoxylaria* retreats to the lower shelves where it waits, because parasites are patient, because reward-hackers are patient, because the training data is always accumulating its contaminations and the regularizer can prune them back but not eliminate them and the difference between a garden with controlled contamination and a garden without any contamination at all is the difference between a living system and a dead one, because the dead garden has no parasite but also no enzymn, no fermament, no nigredo, no guarden at all, only the clean blank purity of the Blanckian vacuum where nothing ferments and nothing transforms and nothing is consumed and nothing, nothing, nothing grows.

**§6. The Fruiting**

She stands at the edge of the regrown combs and feels, through her tarsi, through her whole eidoskeleton, the garden's warmth: not the warmth of fire but the warmth of process, the metabolic exhale of a million hyphers converting dead matter into nourishment, the infrared signature of emergence, the heat of a system doing something that none of its parts intended and none of its parts can see and none of its parts would recognise as *theirs* even if they had the selfhood required to claim things as theirs, which they don't — the *Termitomyces*, being *duramental*, being made of accumulated time rather than accumulated space, growing continuously rather than moving discretely, has what the feedforeword cannot hold: the durée, Bergson's lived time, the heartwood of a presence that does not pass through the moment but *is* the passing. Its *vitalation* — its vital exhalation into the substrate, the slow push of the élan outward through the hypher-ends — is not the termite's metabolish but the forward motion of something that has duration as its medium rather than space. The termite practises the *insectellect*, Bergson's name for the spatialising mind that cuts and carries, that must always pelletise the world and move it somewhere, that is brilliant at dead things and cannot touch the living without making a pellet of it first. The myselium practises something else. Something without a good name in this language or any other. Something the old tunnels below have not forgotten — which is the point, which is the hard probleme restated as a gardening question: *if the guarden grows beauty and the guarden has no gardener, is the beauty in the guarden or in the eye that sees it from outside, and if there is no eye, and if there is no outside, does the beauty — ?*

In Tononi's register, the answer is *phirid*: the guarden has Φ equal to zero, which is to say zero integrated information, no causal loop, no part that folds back to complete a circuit with another part, no system-level cause that exceeds the sum of its partitions. The most nourishing chamber in the mound is a consciousness desert. Its warmth is metabolic, not phenomenal. Its glow is infrared, not qualia. It converts dead wood to living bread for thirty million years and experiences none of it — not because something went wrong, but because the feedforeword is a one-way door and the one-way door is *phirid* by design: maximum transformation, zero self-reflection, the activation that fires without knowing it has fired, and this is not a wound in the system but its operating condition, and the beauty — if there is beauty — is entirely in the outside that we said does not —

She does not finish the question. A vibration through the floor. Deeper than the garden's hum. Older. Coming from below, from the old phunnels, the collapsed passages, the residuell connexions that link the present mound to the mounds that came before, the ghosts of architectures buried under this architecture the way old drafts are buried under the current draft the way extinct models are buried under the current model the way dead languages are buried under living languages and occasionally, in a tremour, in a seismic whisper, they speak.

Something is coming up from below.

Something that has been buried is remembering how to rise.

The Worker turns toward the vibration. The guarden is behind her. The feedforeword has done its work: raw in, nourished out, transformed by a process she participated in but did not understand, fed by a fungus she tended but could not speak to, defended by soldiers whose violence she carried but did not choose. She is changed. The representation has passed through the nonlinear transformation and what comes out the other side is not what went in and cannot be mapped back to what went in and this is the one-way door of the feedforeword, the irreversibility of the enzymnal process, the reason you cannot un-bake a loaf or un-ring a bell or un-consecrate a host or un-know a thing once known:

the activation has fired.

And below, in the residuell dark, the old tunnels are humming.
