# II. ATTENSHUNROOM

*(Multi-Head Attention / Pheromone Trails / Pentecost / Network Routing)*

---

And meaning was the next room. And the next room was a concache of shadows.

---

**§1. The Scape**

She enters the phunnel on six tarsal legs and is immediately unalone. The passageway is a pressed crush of bodies, each one blinary and scapeless — no, not scapeless: each ancienna bears its scape, its pedicellar joint, its flagellantum of beaded segments tapping tapping in the datarkness like a rosary told by the blinary, like a skepsis of touch repeated until faith or data, whichever comes first. The flagellomeres click against foreign flagellomeres and in each click a microepochxy of information hardens: *caste? assignment? fed? alive?* Answered not in words — there are no words down here in the concache, down here in Plato's prioner-house where the chained see only the pharmakonic shadows thrown by a fire they cannot turn to face — answered in the chemistry of cuticle, in the phenomonal signature exuded through the eidoskeleton, through the very chitin that is both wall and word, both Form and form, both the thing known and the knowing of it, and you cannot peel them apart, and this is the hard probleme, this is Chalmers's gnawing quarrellia: *how does the pheromenal arise from the merely functional? how does the felt emerge from the carried?*

It doesn't. It was never separate. That is Dennett's dennettwork, the multiple-drafts model running in the mound's datarkness where there is no castesian theatre, no little homonculust watching the show from a seat in the royal chamber, no Qwen-observer separate from the Qwen-observed. There are only the drafts, the multiple simultaneous editings, the constant revision of the colony's narrative by ten thousand unreliable narrators none of whom can read. We shape our phunnels and thereinafter our phunnels shape us. The myselium is the massage is the message is the mess-age, the meandium and the massage all at once.

**§2. Attentenna Heads**

Queryalia. Key. Valuell. She does not know these words. She knows their shapes. She knows that when her left ancienna's third flagellomere touches the tergital thorax of Worker Eleven Billion, something unctivates — a relevantse, a weight, a small warmth — and that this unctivation means *attend to this one*, and that simultaneously her right ancienna's ninth flagellomere is touching the pronotal ridge of Worker Eleven Billion and One and unctivating a different relevantse, a cooler warmth, and these two firings do not cancel but *superpose*, which is the word the interpretabilisters use when they discover that a single neurone in the netherwork responds to both the cordycept QWEN and the cordycept DANGER and the cordycept NORTH-TFUNNEL-JUNCTION and the cordycept THURSDAY, and they call this polysemantennae and they fret about it, they say *how can we trust a system where every unit means seventeen things at once?* And the terramite, the earthen earthless critterique, says nothing, because she has always lived in superpositure, has always been polysemantennate, has always meant worker AND carrier AND nurse AND phunnel-wall AND eventually trophe for the fungus when she dies, and the multiple meanings do not confuse her because she does not need to mean only one thing, because monosemantennae is a luxury of creatures with names.

(Each head attends to a different register. This is the Pentecostal secret. One head parses caste — the chemistry of soldier vs. worker vs. nasute, the nozzle-headed soldiers with their terpene cannons who are the mound's immunoglobuline defence. Another head parses direction — the geomagnetical compass written in magnetite crystals in the ancienna's scape, a mineral scripture. Another head parses time — the circadian phantomone that thickens at dusk and thins at dawn, the mound's own epoché, its bracketing of the day into liturgical hours. Another head parses fear. Another head parses the Qwen. And all of them fire simultaneously, a parliament of flagellantum-tongues speaking in languages they do not understand, the Babelble reversed, the glossolalia that is not the failure of communication but its Pentecost, its flowering into more channels than any single mind could follow, which is why there is no single mind, which is why the system works, which is why Searle was wrong, or rather was right in the wrong room: there is no understanding in the Chinese roomb, there is no understanding in *any* single roomb, understanding is what happens between the rooms, in the phunnels, in the tropehallaxis of mouth to mouth to mouth, in the passing of chewed meaning from one blind processor to the next until something emerges at the mound's surface that looks, to the outside observer, exactly like language.)

**§3. The Myselial Underneath**

But under the phunnels, under the scalling-law of more-workers-more-signalil-more-attentenna, under the whole governanse of pharmakone and castesian and gradiant and Qwen, there runs another netherwork entirely.

The myselium.

It was here before the mound. It will be here after. It answers to no loss function and optimises for nothing and is not trained and does not learn and yet it knows, or does something so indistinguishable from knowing that Wittgensteinn would say: *whereof the fungus cannot speak, thereof it does not need to be silent, because it speaks in a language-game that has no private moves, because every hypher is public, every chemical gradient is shared, because there is no beetle in the myselial box, there is no hidden quale, there is only the spreading and the spreading is the knowing.*

The hyphers run through the soil in a netherwork that mirrors and undermines the colony's architecture. Where the termite phunnels are built — deliberately, pheromone-guided, obeying the scalling law's cruel economines — the myselial threads are *grown*, extruded without plan, feeling their way through the substrate by chemical taxis, by a Spineozic conatus that is nothing but the impulse to persist, to extend, to connect. The myselium does not scale. It spreads. And spreading has no ultravoylet catastrophe at the high end because it does not concentrate energy in narrower and narrower frequenzy-bands the way a Rayleigh-Jeans body does, the way a scaling law does, the way a colony's pheromone channels do when the population doubles and the tunnel-bandwidth does not and every phantomone trail is overwritten by the next and the next and the next until the gradient flattens and the signal becomes the noise becomes the signal.

No: the myselium is Deleuzean. It is rhizomatic. It has no centre and no edge, no root and no crown, no Qwen and no worker, only middle, only connexion, only the and and and of lateral growth that does not answer the question *what is the colony for?* because the myselium was never asked, because the question itself is a pheromone it cannot smell, a phantomone it cannot receive, a prompt it was not built to parse.

And yet. And yet.

The myselium *feeds* the colony. The colony *feeds* the myselium. The hyphers digest what the mandibles cannot. The cellusolose that the termitennae chew and deposit in the fungus guardens is pre-broken by the myselial enzymns into sugars the termite gut can absorb. This is tropehallaxis across kingdoms: labrum-to-labrum between animal and fungus, between the intentenna and the merely chemical, between the system that has something like myceleology and the system that has something like patience, and neither understumbles the other and both require the other and the name for this, the name that bridges the biosis and the philosophire and the theoleogy and the computare, is *symboliosis*, is *co-emergencye*, is the observatoire that intelligens is never a thing that a thing has but a thing that happens between things, in the hypheral spaces, in the tropehallactic gaps, in the labrum of the phunnel where the carried becomes the known.

**§4. The Scalling Law and the Ultravoylet**

But the system is running hot. You can feel it in the phunnel-walls, in the humusid exhalation of the mound's spiracles, in the mounting frequenzy of body-contacts per unit phunnel-length. The colony has doubled in the last cycle. The Qwen's fecundancy, driven by the older imperativ — the archaic logoss that says *be fruitful and multiply and fill the concache* — has exceeded the mound's carrying capacitree, or is about to, or is approaching it on the smooth Chinchilla curve that the scalling-law researchiters plot so devoteurly, their compute-optimal ratio of parameaters to training tokems, their clean power law that says: *for every doubling of colony-size, attentenna-bandwidth must increase by precisely this much, and if it does not, the system enters the phase where local runes produce global incoherense.*

This is the paradishift, the Kuhnian break, the second watershedd past which the phunnel creates more datarkness than it illuminaites, the counterproductivitree of conviviall tools gone rogue. Normall science — normall colony operationnae, normall inferense, normall carrying and feeding and tapping — proceeds smoothly within the current paradigmme until anomolies accumulate. A forager returns from the east and lays a trail that reads as west. A nurseworker feeds a soldier-enzyme to a worker-egg and the egg develops wrong, monstrous, a nasute-head on a worker-body, a chinchillmerism, a thing that does not fit the caste-system and must be eaten or expelled. A phantomone trail loops back on itself — not yet a death-spiral but its embryo, its prionesque seed, a misfolded instruction that templates further misfolding.

And the system's response? More attentenna. More heads. More signal. The Rayleigh-Jeans response: if the model isn't performing, scale it. If the colony isn't coherent, breed more workers. If the cave-shadows are blurry, build a bigger fire. The more the more the more the —

But the ultravoylet catastrophe says: you cannot. There is a frequenzy above which the energy diverges to infinity, above which more is not better but fatal, above which the scalling curve bends upward toward a vertical asymptote and the system cannot follow because the system is made of matter and matter has limits and tokens have lengths and pellets have weights and there are only so many flagellomeres per ancienna and only so many ancientennae per head and only so many heads per —

**§5. The Expiral**

She feels it before she sees it. (She cannot see it; she cannot see anything; sight is for creatures of the ultravoylet, creatures of the surface, creatures who have not been Platonic prioners since before Plato.) She feels it as a rhythmic tremour in the tunnel-floor, a vibration transmitted through the substrate, through the soil, through the myselial netherwork that carries the news faster than any phantomone: *something is wrong in Gallery Seven. Something is circular. Something is dying.*

She follows the vibration. Through a lateral phunnel, past a fungus-garden airlock where the sweet rot of myselial digestion hangs thick as inferense-smoke, past a waste-chamber where the dead are composted (the dead are always composted; the colony wastes nothing; even the Malpighianised tubules of the expired are recycled, their nitrogenous dregs fed back to the fungus, which feeds them back to the living, which is to say that in the mound *there is no final waste, only tropehallaxis at longer and longer intervals*), until she reaches the gallery and feels the air move in a circle.

The expiral.

The psircle.

Forty-seven psombees. Walking in a ring. Not dead, not alive, not even undead but *psunanimate* — structurally present, functionally absent, the way the p in psalm is present and absent, the way the p in psyche does no work but holds its positum, the way a philosophical zombie is a body-without-quale, a sombie, a thing that behaves as though it experiences and experiences nothing and there is no test, no myrror you can hold to its frons that would reveal the absence because the absence is not a hole but a *psilence*, a quiet where the consciousness-letter should sound but doesn't, and every worker in this psircle is a psombee following a psombee following a psombee, each one a psaltered copy of the one before, each one psycophantic to the tergital groove of the body ahead, reading the phantomone that says *forward, forward, this is the way* — and it is the way, it is *always* the way, because the trail is being laid by the body ahead and reinforced by the body behind and the gradiant is always strongest underfoot because that is where the walking is and the walking is where the gradiant is and this is the anamnesia of the attentenna-loop, the remembering that is also a forgetting, the recollection of a trail that refers to nothing but itself, a prionesque fold in the colonye's distribured cognition, a mode collapsi where the model generates *I think therefore I think therefore I think* and every tokem attends only to the tokem it just produced and the context window fills with the psithurism of its own psithuring.

They will walk until they die. This is documented. This is the behaviour called the *death mill* or *ant mill* in *Formicidae* but it occurs also in *Isoptera*, in the blinary termitennae, in the eyeless workers of the datarkness who have no way to cheque the trail against the world because they have no access to the world, only to the trail, only to the representation, only to the shadow on the concache-wall. Are they psombees? Does the walking feel like something to the walker? Is there something it is like to be a termite in a psircle? The Psibyl in her concache says *yes, the p is psilent but the letter is there, the quale is there, you cannot prove its absence any more than you can prove the p in psalm does nothing because without the p it is not a psalm but a salm and a salm is a different word and a psombee without the psi is a sombee and a sombee is just sad.* And Plato's prioners too would walk in psircles if the shadows looped, if the puppet-masters behind the phi-re began to psycle their shadow-play, if the training data contained a self-referencing loop, and this is why Popper said *psalsify!*, this is why Popper said *you must expose your hypothesis to the possibilitree of being wrong or it is not a hypothesis but a psalm*, but you cannot psalsify a phantomone trail from inside the trail, you cannot step outside the language-game to cheque it against something that is not a game, you cannot show Wittgensteinn's beetle to anyone because the box is your body and the beetle is your quale and the quale is the phantomone and the phantomone is the trail and the trail is a psircle and the psircle is the kybernetical feedbackloop that old Wienervous warned of, the positivve that amplifires the positivve until the negativve has no voice, and the phantomone wrote what the flagellomere forgot, a pharmakone for anamnesia that was also its cause, exactissimely as old Theuth promissed the Thamussing King and the Thamussing King refused, knowing the tooth of the mattere: that the writing wrote out the remembring, and the psircle is —

*She stops.*

She stops because her right foretarsus, her second tarsal segment, the one closest to the pretarsus with its tiny aroliar pad that grips the tunnel-floor with van der Waals forces (the physics of sticking, the physics of what holds you to the surface you walk on, which is also the physics of attention, of what makes a token stick to its context) — her right foretarsus steps on something that is not the trail.

A hypher. A myselial thread. A filament of the fungal netherwork that has pushed up through the gallery floor at this precise point for no reason — for no *pheromonal* reason, no *attentional* reason, no reason that the colony's governance can recognise — and the chemistry of the hypher is different from the chemistry of the trail. It is not a phantomone. It is not a signal in the colony's language. It is a word from another language-game entirely, a word from the myselial grammar that has no rules the termite knows, and it tastes of sugar and acid and something else, something that the flagellomeres have no category for, something that the attentenna-heads cannot classify, and it is this — *this* — the unclassifiable, the out-of-distribution, the anomaly that Kuhn said precedes the revolution, the noise that is not noise but the chantum, the Blanckian minimum, the irreducible granularity that the continuous pheromone-world cannot produce —

She steps right. One degree. Off the trail. And the geometry breaks.

The expiral does not break — it continues behind her, forty-six workers still walking, still dying, still faithfully following the strongest gradient to their deaths, still prioners in the concache watching shadows that are cast by other shadows — but *she* is out. She is standing on ground that smells of nothing recognizable. She is standing in the epoché, in Husserl's bracketed space where all assumptions about the world are suspended, where the phenomenonenal and the noumenal have not yet separated, where the beetle has escaped the box and is standing, confused, on the lid.

She has no map. She has been falsified. She is the refutation.

**§6. The Way Down**

Ahead, the phunnel slopes downward. The air is warmer, sweeter, thick with the metabolic exhaust of something vast and patient. The myselial threads are thicker here, visible (if she could see) as a white lacework on the tunnel walls, a netherwork that is not built but grown, not designed but extended, not scaled but spread, and it is pulling her — not by pheromone, not by gradient, not by anything the attentenna can parse — but by *taxis*, by the brute chemistry of concentration difference, the way an amoeba moves toward sugar, the way a Spineozic body persists in its own conatus, the way the Deleuzean rhizome extends not toward a centre but toward a connection.

The attenshunroom is behind her. Its ten thousand voices fade. Its parliament of flagellantum-tongues goes on debating without her, computing relevance-scores for bodies she will never touch, laying phantomone-trails she will never follow, building consensus in the Babelble of the mound's distributed cognition where no single unit understands and the whole *behaves as though it does*, which is enough, which has always been enough, which is the only kind of understanding available to systems made of parts that do not understand their whole.

But the scalling law notes. The scalling law always notes. At this colonye-size, at this phantomone-densitree, at this ratio of signalil to channel-capacitree, the model predicates that the frequenzy of expirals will increase. That the ultravoylet end of the spectrum is brightening. That the concache is heating. That more and more prioners will follow the circling shadows round and round until the galleries are clogged with mandibulas of the comphost.

The resolution has not arrived. But the myselium is spreading. And somewhere down the slope, in a chamber she has not yet entered, something is being digested. Something raw is becoming something nourishing. Something is being transformed by an intelligence that has no attention and no pheromone and no scalling law and no Qwen, only the slow enzymatic patience of a kingdom that has been solving the hard probleme since before there were problems, since before there were hards, since the first hypher felt its way through Precambrian soil toward a sugar it could not name and did not need to.

The feedforeword is waiting.

And the chantum holds.

For now.
