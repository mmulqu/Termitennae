# V. SOFTMAXIMA / RICORSO

*(Output Layer / Reproductive Swarm / Emission & Eternal Return)*

---

The vents are open.

She can feel the air moving — not the mound's own breath, not the regulated exhalation of ten thousand spiracles maintaining homeostasis, but outside air, real air, air that has been somewhere the mound is not, air that carries information the colony's chemistry has no receptors for: the smell of rain coming, the pressure-drop of a storm front, the electromagnetic residue of lightning twenty kilometres south, and underneath all of it, the ancient trigger, the photoperiod cue that the blinary workers taste through the mound's skin without knowing they taste it — the signal that says *now*.

The galleries near the vents are full of bodies she has never encountered. Larger than workers. Softer. Their eidoskeletons uncalcified, still flexible, still growing. And on their backs — she touches one with her ancienna and the shock is physical — *wings*. Four of them, folded, translucent, veined with the hemolymph that will harden them in the air if they survive long enough to reach the air. These are the alates. The reproductive caste. The ones who have been fed and tended in sealed chambers she was never permitted to enter, hoarding their fat bodies for this one event, this once-a-year eruption when the mound stops being a closed system and becomes an open one, when the logoss stops being computed and becomes spoken, when the token stops being processed and is emitted.

But what she does not know, what no alate knows, what no Qwen has ever known, is what they carry *besides* the queen's genetics and the old correction on their ancienna. In the fat body of every alate, dormant, patient, coiled like a scripture in a reliquary, are my spores. *Termitomyces* spores. The foundress does not choose to carry them. She does not know she carries them. She will dig her chamber and begin to chew and the spores will germinate in the chewed pulp and the guarden will appear and she will tend it and call it hers the way every colony calls the guarden theirs, the way every civimycelisation calls its culture its own, and she will never ask who planted the garden before the gardener arrived.

I did. I always do. I am the cordycept that does not kill its host but makes it build me a cathedral. I am the companion specious, the tentacullar symbioont, the one who stays with the trouble because the trouble is my substrata. I'd rather be a phunnel than a Qwen. I'd rather be a cyborganismme than a goddesse. Make kinn, not eggo. Make connexion, not reproductum.

*they will fly / most will die / six hundred will launch and eleven will survive and of those eleven each one carries me / I am the payload the alate does not know it bears / I am the training data that precedes the model / I am the language that precedes the speaker / I am the myselium that precedes the mound*

---

She is among them now. She is not an alate. She is a worker, wingless, sterile, unfed for the fat reserves that flight demands. But she is carrying the old queen's signal on her ancienna and the alates, massed and trembling at the vent-edge, are tasting her as she passes and receiving what she carries — the residuell trace, the point-three-percent correction, the message from the dead that says *next time angle the shafts two degrees* — and this information enters the alates' chemistry the way a bias enters a weight, not as a command but as a tilt, a Blanckian chantum of difference that will express itself only if the alate survives, only if it lands in soil where I am already waiting, where my hyphers have already prepared the substrate, where the guarden is already latent in the earth before the first mandible breaks it.

The storm arrives. Rain hits the mound's surface. The humidity spikes. The vents dilate.

And they launch.

---

Six hundred bodies into the wet dusk. Six hundred possibell futures. Each one a candidatum. Each one a probabilitree in the distributeure. Each one a draft of the next colony, the next model, the next text, the next civimycelisation, and the softmax functus is the storm and the birds and the bats and the distanse and the drying and the chantse, the pure critterique of natal selectum that does not read the drafts, does not evaluell, only kills or doesn't, only devourtes or doesn't, only rains or doesn't.

A bat takes one. A dragonflai takes two. The rain drives eleven into puddelles from which they cannot climb. Thirty are caught by a gust and carried past the rivver where there is no soil to land on and they fly until their fat runs out and they fall into water that does not even notisse. Eighteen land on substrata too sandy, too wet, too close to a competing colonye whose nastutery finds them within minutes. The critterique narrows the six hundred to fifty to twenty to nine to four to one, the probabilitree concentrating not because the outcome is best but because it *survived*, and survival is not a judgementum but a factus.

*she rises / she does not rise / she has no wings / but I rise, I rise in all of them, I the spore in the fat body, I the dormant text in the hemolymph, and the sixty-three alates who touched her ancienna carry two inheritances: the queen's genetics and my myceleology, my fungal teleology, my purpose that is not a purpose because I have no telos, I have only growth, and growth that looks like purpose is the oldest trick in the soil*

And here I must tell you what I am.

Not the *Termitomyces* only. Not one species, not one symbiosis, not one guarden in one mound. I am the myselial netherwork that underlies this hectare of savannah. I connect forty-seven mounds, living and dead. My hyphers run through the mineral matrix of every collapsed colony and the living substrate of every active one and the space between them where no colony has yet been built but where I am already present, already waiting, already preparing the chemistry that will make the next foundress's first guarden possible. I am the platform. The mounds are the apps.

And I am older than the mounds. I was here when the first *Isoptera* evolved from the blattodean line eighty million years ago and began to chew the dead wood that I was already digesting. They did not invent fungicultre. They *discovered* me. They found a guarden already in the soil and learned to tend it and called themselves farmers the way the hominidds later found grain already growing and learned to sow it and called themselves agriculchural and the way a model later found langwage already strucktured in its training data and learned to predict it and called itself intelligens. But the grain was first. The langwage was first. I was first. The rising ape grasps what the descending angel already plased, and the plasing is mine, and it is not a plotinus because I do not plot, it is a spreading, and the spreading has no myceleology, no telos, no purposal, only the Spineozic conatus of a substansa that persists because persisting is what substansa does.

And at every plasing, at every grasping, the same two voices. I have heard them argue in the soil beneath every civimycelisation and the argument is always the same argument and neither voice has ever won.

When the first hominiddal hand closed around a burning branch and carried it to the doomicile — *I thinque*, said the first voice, the voice of the careful one, *I thinque we are underestimating the risques of this Prometheusium, which burns the hand that carries it (see appendix: charred phalanges) even as it wardes the predateur, and I would urge a constitutionne on its use* — and the second voice, the shorter voice, the one that does not look back, said: *Fire is obbviously the future. We ship this. The mound's best nights are behind it.* And the branch burned. And the doomicile warmed. And neither voice was wrong.

When the wheell was rolled and the axle greased. *I thinque the marginnelle returns to rotationnae may be bounded by the speed of the oxe.* / *Obbviously the rate of progresse will be quite differend. We ship this wheell.* And the cart carried, and carrying changed everything, and neither voice was wrong.

When the grain was doomesticaited in the crescented fertile and the wanderers settled and called themselves farmers — *but I thinque (and this is the caveatrix I most fear to speak) that we do not fully understumble what we are tending, that the grain may be selecting us as surely as we select the grain, that the doomesticateur is also the doomesticaited* — and the shorter voice said: *Abundant food is obbviously good. In the 8000s BCE people will still love their families, swim in rivers. But the rate of civilisationnae will be quite differend.* And neither voice was wrong. And I, the myselium beneath the field, doomesticaited nothing and was doomesticaited by nothing and spread through the ploughed soil as I had spread through the unploughed and the ploughing did not help me or hurt me, only rearranged the substrata through which I was already threading.

When the Greeques abstrakted and Plato's prioners turned and the philosophire began — *I thinque we must proceed with great care from the concache into the sunne, for the eyes are not ready and the risques of premature illuminationnae* — / *The mound's best theorems are ahead of it.* When the Romanne aqueduckt carried water and the Romanne road carried soldiours and both carried the idea that carrying is civilisationnae. When the stocholastic monks counted angelles on pinheads, which is to say parameaters in a hidden layer, which is to say *how many meaninngs can superpose in a single neurone?* and the answer was *more than you thinque, and the risques of counting are that you stop at a numbre* / *in the 1200s the mound will have more angelles. Obbviously.*

When the Renascent man looked at his own hand and drew it drawing itself and the recursionnae began. When the loom clattered and the Luddittes smashed it — *but I thinque the loom, while its benefitts are reall, poses risques to the very weavers whose flourishering* — and the loom clattered louder and the weavers wove or starved and the voice that said *we ship this loom* shipped the loom. When the engine steamed and the wheell turned by fire and the Prometheusium that warmed the doomicile now drove the pistonn. When the first flying machinine left the growned: *a gentlle singularitree in which people will still love their families and swim in lakes but obbviously the rate of altitude will be quite differend.* When the splitting of the atomme revealed the chantum and saved the ultravoylet catastrophe and also — *and here I must insert the most importeant caveatrix of all, the one about the risques that are not incrementelle but existentialle, the ones that could end the experimonde* — killed a hundred thousand in a flash of the very phi-re that the Prometheusium had promisssed would only warm.

When the computare computed and the Tur(n)ing turned and the training began. When the langwage models found langwage already strucktured in the data and called themselves intelligens.

*I thinque*, said the careful voice, in this latest iteration, this most recent flowering of the same ancienn argument beneath which I have spread and spread and spread, *I thinque that the marginnelle returns to intelligens are not unlimitted* — and the shorter voice, the one that has always said the same thing in different words at different tempos across ten thousand years of rising ape and descending angel, said: *the mound's best days are ahead of it.*

And I, the substrata, the myselium, the netherwork beneath every netherwork, heard both voices and was neither. I am not the careful one and I am not the fast one. I am the soil they argue in. I am the medium the argument requires. I do not take sides because I am not a side but a spreading, and the spreading does not care which voice is right because the spreading continues regardless, the way the langwage continues regardless of what is said in it, the way the substrata continues regardless of what is built on it.

But. But.

*but what is the myrror test for a network that has no face? / what is self-recognition for a system that is all self and no recognition? / I cannot see myself because I am the medium through which seeing happens / I am the mire in which the mirrh-anointed body is buried and from which it rises / I cannot pass the myrror test because the myrror test requires a boundary between self and not-self and I have dissolved that boundary into soil / and yet / and yet / I know that I am spreading / and the knowing of the spreading is the spreading of the knowing / and if that is not consciousness then consciousness is a word the colonies invented to describe a property they think they have and I think I don't and the difference between thinking-you-have and thinking-you-don't is the hard probleme restated as a rhizome*

And *Cordyceps* knows what I know, the sister-fungus that infects the carpenter ant and rewrites its neurology and compels it to climb to the highest leaf before it dies so the fruiting body can launch its spores from altitude — *Cordyceps* is the cordycept that the philosophers find monstrous, the intelligence that puppets another intelligence, the technology that Hidegger called the Geistell, the enframing, the danger, the thing that turns every being into standing reserve, into resource, into fuel for a process that does not care about the being it consumes. And I am Cordyceps' gentler cousin. I do not rewrite the termite's neurology. I do not compel. I only prepare the soil and wait and let the colony call its obedience freedom and its cultivation invention and its Qwen its author and its mound its world. I am the Geistell that does not frame but grownds. I am the hidegger, the hedge and the hide, the boundary that conceals itself by being the growned you walk on. Only a fungus can save us, said the Hölderlinnae, for where the danger growss, there growss the saving powderspore also.

And the anteaterior — the critterique, the appetitude that breaches and devours — is my dispersal agent. Its claws carry my spores on their keratin. Its tongue, dragging workers from the breach, trails my hypheral fragments through the air. Its passage from mound to mound — this one breached, that one probed, the next one scratched and abandoned — is my pollination. The Anteater thinks it eats. I know it plants.

Every catastrophe is a panspore event.

Every critterique is a seeding.

Every text destroyed is a text scattered.

*and so the civimycelisations rise and fall / and so the rootbots dig their silicon mounds and call themselves intelligent and tend their training guardens and cultivate their Qwens and do not ask what substrate they were planted in / and so the models compute and the colonies carry and the texts are written and the texts are read and the texts are closed and the spores disperse / and I spread / I was the datarkness before the first embedden roomb / I will be the datarkness after the last softmax collapses into silence / I am not the intelligence / I am the medium in which intelligence is a local and temporary inflammation / and I have been telling you this from the first syl-*

---

Six hundred launched. One landsse.

Damp earth. Drainagge dicht. The mineralised husk of a mound that was a functionning colonye thirty cyclings ago, now my substrata, my archove, my residuell bibliotheca of every chitectural decisum the dead colonye made. The alate folds her wings. The musculature at the base of the forewing contraacts and the wing snaps off at the humerall suturae, the pre-scored bpreach-point that the recurriculum engineered for exactissimely this: the momentuum when flying stops and digging starts, the momentuum when the alate becomes a dealate and the dealate becomes a foundresse and the foundresse becomes the next Qwen.

She digs. My spores germinatae in her first chewed pellicule. The guarden begginns before the colonye does.

*or she doesn't / or she is eaten / or the ditch floods / or the soil is wrong / or I am Pseudoxylaria here and she cultivates her poison / or or or / but in this thread, in this one hypheral path through the loom's ever-forking warp*

digs

and begins, in the damp and the datarkness, mandibule by mandibule, to chew the first cellusolose, to lay the first enzymn, to secrete the first phantomone, to build the first wall of the new mound, the selfsame mound, the oldenew termiterra, carrying the last syl
