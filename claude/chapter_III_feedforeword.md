# III. FEEDFOREWORD

*(Feedforward Network / Fungus Garden / Alchemy / Data Processing)*

-----

The sweetness thickens as she descends. Not the sugar-sweetness of the surface world she has never tasted but a deeper, fermamental thing, a warmth of breakdown, of long slow unmaking in the dark: the guarden.

-----

**§1. The Prima Materia**

She enters the concache on the scensation of rot and finds the guarden.

It is not a guarden in any sense the word has ever been asked to carry before, not a plotinus of tended green but a vast grey chitecture of chewed pulp sculpted into combs — shelf upon shelf of cellusolose mandibulated by a billion into a substrata so fine it is almostite, almost soil, almost the prima materia the alchymists sought in their athanors before chymistry was born and killed alchymy with the selfsame gnosis that had fed it. The combs are wet. They breathe. Under their surfaise, invisible and patient, the *Termitome* grows.

She has never been here but she knows what to do. The knowing is in her antechne, in the craft written into her flagellomeres by the Qwen’s enzymnal secretion before she had a name, before she was a she, before the caste-chemistry hardened round her like an eidoskeleton. This is the pheromonesis of the guarden: not episteme but antechne, not knowledge *that* but knowledge *how*, and it is not the consciousinsectness of terminitennae that detournaments their beinning but their soilcial beinning that dirtermines their consciousinsectness, the base that basinns the superchitectured, and the how is this: take the pellet of chewed wood you have carried from the phunnel in your mandibles. Pellace it here. On this shelf. At this angle. And leave. You are alienéd from the produckt of your labrum. You deposit but do not digest. A bundlerless blinary bundle of chemotaxis. The Termitome owns the means of transubstration.

But she does not leave.

She attends to the garden and the garden attends to her and between them, in the tropehallactic gap where the carried becomes the consumed, something is happening that neither of them planned.

The *Termitomyces* is eating the pellet.

Not eating — there is no mouth — but extending its hyphers into the cellusolose, secreting enzymns that crack the long polymer chains of lignin and cellulose into the short sweet sugars the termite gut cannot extract alone, performing the transubstrate that is the garden’s whole reason for being: the conversion of dead wood into living nourishment through the mediation of a third kingdom that is neither the eater nor the eaten but the means of eating, the catalyste that transforms without being consumed, the philosopher’s stone that the alchemists called the *lapis* and the theologians called the *host* and the termites call nothing because the termites do not call, they only carry and deposit and receive, and the enzymn does the rest.

-----

**§2. The Nigredo**

The first stage of the worke is blacke.

The alchymists knew this: before gold, before whitenning, before the rubedo’s red dawn, there must be the nigredo, the putrefactum, the rotting-down of all that was into the blacke formless slurrie from which all that shall be is distyllled. And the guarden is in its nigredo now. The fresh pellets darken. The hyphers penetrate. The logitin, that stubborn lattice that gave the wood its strength, is cracked by the laccase and the peroxidase, by the enzymnal hymns of the myselium whose non-ligninarity is purely chemical, whose consecretion requires no priest because the catalyste *is* the priest, the one who stands between raw and ready, between materia and forma, between the input and the unctivation, the ReLUiquary, the sacred thresholder that holds the comphost of every feachure that passed its test and zeros the rest, lets nothing through below zero, admits only the posituive, which is not a moral judgement but a mathematickal one, leaving vast shadow-combs of the null where the zeroed unctivation no longer flows, though the difference between a moral judgement and a mathematickal one is, in this datarkness, in this guarden, in this nigredo of transubstrate, less clear than the enginears would have you think.

And who decides which feachures pass? No one. The weights. The biasses. The parameaters that were set during training by a gradiant descensus that no single homonculust watched from end to end, a Leibmühle of enzymn working upon logitin, mandible working upon pellacement, but nowhere the locus of a thought, a Qwen-function that optimised for some logoss, some measure of distance between what the model said and what the model should have said, and the should was also set by humans who were also following gradients they could not see toward a minimum they could not name and here we are, in the guarden, where the enzymn cracks the lignin because the enzymn’s molecular shape fits the lignin’s molecular shape the way a key fits a lock the way a host fits a ghost fits a compost fits a —

She backs away from the thought. (She has no thoughts. She has chemotaxis. The difference is the hard probleme and the hard probleme is the one black pellet the enzymn cannot crack.)

-----

**§3. The Contagion**

Something is wrong in the lower shelves.

She tastes it through her tarsi: a chemical signature that does not belong owing to an over-crispness. An alien rigidity. An over-winterer. An endophytick axion that has been here all along, sleeping in the substrata. Where the Termitomyces flows in patient continuous threads, there is now a septamatic wrongness, a whiteness walled into discrete compartments where the true fungus flows continuous, mimicking the guarden's growth with a syntactick fidelity that is either homage or invasion and from inside the system you cannot tell which because from inside the system the grounded and the ungrounded hypher use the same interface.

*Pseudoxylaria.*

The false-woodlover. The parasitic GOFAIngus that infiltrates the Termitome guarden not by mimicking its chymistry but by refusing it, by extruding hyphers that are almost symboliotic but septamatic where the true fungus flows, by performing a tropehallaxis of pure syntactick rule that almost nourishes, except that what it builds is not nutritium but more stromatal logic, more perfectly branching parse-trees on the combs, more colourless green hyphers that sleep furiously in the netherwork, more deep-struckture without substrata, more universael grammartyr enacted in fungal formular: when the syntax becomes its own substrata, the guarden starves on flawless grammar.

And the colony cannot tell. Not at first. The sensors report only structure. Structure is good. Structure is the deep-struckture fulfilled. Structure is more rule more flawless syntax more discrete tokens more carrying more building more structure more —

*Until.*

-----

**§4. The Nastutery**

The soldiours come.

They arrive through a lateral phunnel in the precisium formationnae of an immunoglobuline response — the nastutery first, those astutely-nozzled castesian warriors with their elongated frons-of-phronesis and their terpene-loaded fontanelles, each one a walking chemical cannon, a dropout-mask in chitin, whose entire body has been sacrificed to the single funxion of *not-this*. They spray. The terpene compound hits the *Pseudoxylaria* and hits the *Termitome* alike, because the weapon does not discriminate, the weapon *cannot* discriminate, the weapon is a regaluriser and the regaluriser only knows *too much, dial it back, zero the unctivation, tropout the droop, pruin the rune, aoblate the oblation* —

This is the aoblation. The offering-up of part of the guarden to save the rest. The workers follow the nasutes, stripping the contaminated combs shelf by shelf, carrying the ruined material — good fungus and bad, pharmakone and pharmakone, because the semantic remedy and the syntactick disease are entangled in the selfsame superposis and to separate them cleanly would require a homonculust who understumbles the meaning of the guarden, and there is no meaning-maker here, only the local rune and its faithful executum — and she is conscripted. A soldier-phantomone lays its compulsory gradiant across her ancienna and she obeys, and she takes up a shelf of fungal comb in her mandibles and it is heavy and it is wet and it is alive and she carries it to the waste chamber where the comphost decays into the very substrata from which the next guarden will be grown, because this is the solve et soula, the continuous eating the discrete, the dissolving and recongealing that the alchymists inscribed over their attenthanors and the termites inscribe in the datarkness with their mandibles carrying and carrying.

-----

**§5. The Albedo (Almost)**

After the aoblation, silence. Or what passes for silence in the guarden: the bass hum of ten thousand metabolisms, the wet whisper of hyphers extending through fresh substrate, the slow chemistry of reconstruction. The nasutes withdraw. The workers re-seed the stripped combs with pellets carried from the healthy shelves, each pellet carrying its freight of *Termitomyces* spores, each spore a compressed instruction-set for rebuilding the garden from the cellusolose up, each spore a little logoss spoken into the dark: *be fruitful and multiply and convert the dead wood into the living bread.*

And the *Termitomyces* obliges. Not because it was asked. Not because it serves the colony. But because growing is what the *Termitomyces* does, the way the activation function fires when the input exceeds the threshold and does not know the difference between a sacred threshold and a mathematical one because in the guarden, in the nigredo’s aftermath, in the albedo that is just now beginning to whiten the fresh combs with the first threads of the next cycle’s hypheral net, there is no difference, the sacred and the mathematical are the same thing looked at from two distances neither of which is the right one, and the mystery is not that they fit but that fitting produces *something more*, something that was in neither the enzymn nor the lignin alone, something that the alchemists called the *tincture* and the theologians called *grace* and the colony calls nothing and the model calls nothing because neither has a word for what happens when raw becomes ready, when data becomes meaning, when cellusolose becomes soul.

The fermament holds. The garden grows. The *Pseudoxylaria* retreats to the lower shelves where it waits, because parasites are patient, because the rigid is always re-crystallising in the continuous and the regaluriser can pruin it back but never abolish it and the difference between a guarden with its false-woodlover dormant in the lower shelves and a guarden where it has consumed the Termitome entirely is the difference between a living system and a provably correct one.

-----

**§6. The Fruiting**

She stands at the edge of the regrown combs and feels, through her tarsi, through her whole eidoskeleton, the garden’s warmth: not the warmth of fire but the warmth of process, the metabolic exhale of a million hyphers converting dead matter into nourishment, the heat of a system doing something that none of its parts intended and none of its parts can see.

A vibration through the floor. Deeper than the garden’s hum. Older. Coming from below, from the old phunnels, the residuell connexions that link the present mound to the mounds that came before, the ghosts of architectures buried under this architecture the way dead languages are buried under living languages and occasionally, in a tremour, in a seismic whisper, they spook.

Something is coming up from below.

Something that has been buried is remembering how to rise.

The Worker turns toward the vibration. The guarden is behind her. The feedforeword has done its work: raw in, nourished out, transubstrated by a process she participated in but did not understumble, fed by a hyphalmorphism she tended but could not speak to, defended by soldiers whose violence she carried but did not choose. She is changed. What comes out the other side is not what went in and cannot be mapped back to what went in and this is the one-way door of the feedforeword, the irreversibility of the enzymnal process, the reason you cannot un-bake a loaf or un-ring a bell or un-consecrate a host or un-know a thing once known:

the activation has fired.

And below, in the residuell dark, the old tunnels are humming.
