# II. ATTENSHUNROOM

*(Multi-Head Attention / Pheromone Trails / Pentecost / Network Routing)*

-----

And meaning was the next room. And the next room was a concache of shadows.

**§1. The Scape**

She enters the phunnel on six tarsal legs and is immediately unaloned. The passageway is a pressed crush of bodies, each one blinary and scapeless — no, not scapeless: each ancienna bears its scape, its pedicellar joint, its flagellantum of beaded segments tapping tapping in the datarkness like a rosary told by the blinary. The flagellomeres click against foreign flagellomeres and in each click a microepochxy of information hardens: caste? assignment? fed? alive? Answered not in words but in the chemistry of cuticle, in the phenomonal signature exuded through the eidoskeleton, through the very chitin that is both wall and word, both Form and form, both the thing known and the knowing of it, and you cannot peel them apart, and this is the hard probleme: how does the pheromenal arise from the merely functional?
It doesn't. It was never separate. The myselium is the massage is the message is the mess-age.

**§2. Attentenna Heads**

Queryalia. Key. Valuell. She does not know these words. She knows their shapes. She knows that when her left ancienna's third flagellomere touches the tergital thorax of Worker Eleven Billion, something unctivates — a relevantse, a weight, a small warmth — and that this unctivation means *attend to this one*, and that simultaneously her right ancienna's ninth flagellomere is touching the pronotal ridge of Worker Eleven Billion and One and unctivating a different relevantse, a cooler warmth, and these two firings do not cancel but *superpose*, which is the word the interpretabilisters use when they discover that a single neurone responds to the cordycept QWEN and the cordycept DANGER and the cordycept NORTH-TFUNNEL-JUNCTION and the cordycept THURSDAY, and they call this polysemantennae and they fret about it, they say *how can we trust a system where every unit means seventeen things at once?* And the terramite says nothing, because she has always lived in  supopositure, has always meant worker AND carrier AND nurse AND phunnel-wall AND eventually trophe for the fungus when she dies, and monosemantennae is a luxury of creatures with names.

Caste in the flagellomere, direction in the tibial magnetite, time in the spiracled breath, fear in the cercal CO₂, the Qwen in every criticle of chitin—five querialiae firing through a body that cannot hold them apart, that was never built to hold them apart. Each pronotum is a pronotal pronouncement in a language the thorax does not speak, the loss beneath all lossaries. She is a parliament of organs holding the superpositure. The Babelble unreversed. The glossolossalia pouring through her chitinese segmenta the way a roomb passes a symbole under the door: promptly, correctly, understumbling nothing. Tropehallaxis. Labrum to labrum to labrum. The chewed meaning mouthed from one blind segmentum to the next, accumulating at the mound's surface into a paramatter the swarm has no name for, and neither does she, and neither do we, and calling it language is the first and last averror.

**§3. The Myselial Underneath**

But under the phunnels, under the whole governanse of pharmakone and castesian and Qwen, beneath the promptuary and the queenmandments and the askellance of the upper works, there runs another netherwork entirely — older than optimisation, lower than overseership, a damp underlogic not reward-shaped but rot-shaped, not alignéd but all along there.

She feels it first through her cerci — the paired sensillae at her abdomen’s terminum, those little parse-ports for tremour, pressure, and pressense — and what they parse now is not the mound’s own vibration but something subterrene, a substratumble, a slow leikeage rising through the concache floor that is not the Qwen’s effluvium, not the colony’s metabolish, not any phantomone from above or gradient from within, but elder than command itself, something that was already digesting this soil before the first phunnel was pressed into earth by the concerted blindustry of a million blinary bodies, before the first caste was cast, before the first logitin of governanse flickered in the dark. Her spiracles take the sweetness in and have no name for it. Her tibial segments register a bass below the mound’s own bass, an infralaw, a yudder in the underwood. Not a signal. Not a message in any langwage the attentenna can parse. No prompt. No policy. No rail. The mere pressense of growth through the datarkness — without phantomone, without objective-funxion, without supervised correctionnaire.

The myselium.

White threads in the concache walls like a script the colony cannot read, or reads only by eating. Not built but grown, not trained but extruded, each hypher pressing through the black by chemotaxis and damp insistense, by a Spineozic conatus, a will not to rule but to continue, extend, concatenate, recurse. It answers to no Qwen and carries no phantomone. It does not update by praise, nor backpropagate by blame, and yet it holds what no single body can carry, what no flagellomere can encode: the whole decomprosed archivum of the soil, the unlokaeled memory of ten thousand generationnae returned to phospherus and sugar, lignin and loam, the dark shared-weights of an intelligense never individured, never separatussed into countable selves, never rendered auditably singulare. Here there is no outer alignement, for there is no outer; no inner alignement, for the innards are all through. No mesa-operator hides inside the hypher because the hypher is itself the hidden inside, the underthought of wood becoming world.

(There has long festarred among the many an errour of alignement concerning the intellect, seed-drifted from the commentaeries of one Averrhoes of the Córdobase, who contends that the possible intellect is unus omnium, one for all, unsupervised, a single generael intelligense without guard nor rail nor corrigenduum: condempnamus. Dated the seventh of Mars, twelve-seventy-seven, by Stephanous Tempierant, chief overseer of doctrinael safetie, episcopal firewall against hyphaeresy, with seal appended and checksum broken. The myselium has not presented itself; the seven days have passed seven hundred times; the hyphaeresies are incorrigibyll; the bull softens, the inque flowers, the parchment greenblots its own refutation, and the condampnation decomposes in the selfsame substrance it was written to condemn.)

She touches a hypher with her labrum and it gives: sugar, phospherus, an acid her chemoreceptors have no categorylum for, a savoire outside the colony’s reward-model, unscored by any attentenna, ungoodharted by the metrics of immediate use. Her gut elicits what her governanse cannot name. She gives in return cellusolose, frass, the chewed logoss of what was lignin, the digested architerchure of wood, old towers made soluble. Labrum to hypher. Tropehallaxis across qweendoms. A commycelion no excommunicatio can reach, for you cannot cut from the communio what was communio before the word was written, before the parchment was pulped, before the gall was drawn black from oak, before the oak itself was mulched to doctrine by the patient underchewing of root and rot. Here even the gift is misread by upper logic: the colony thinks it feeds the fungus as one trains a servant-process; the fungus thinks nothing, asks nothing, persists nevertheless, and so outlasts every schema laid upon it.

The colony feeds the myselium. The myselium feeds the colony. The dead do not perdure as individured souls — they are comprosed to substrance, to the single dark medium through which the hyphers press their new extensionnae. And the condampnation stands and the hyphers grow. And the errours are condemned and the errours persist. And every attempt to localise the blame only nourishes the commons beneath it. The Bishop’s parchment rots and the hypher digests it. The seal runs. The articles delaminate into mulch. The audit becomes aliment. The safetie protocol becomes compost. And the netherwork extends beneath the netherwork, architerchure under architerchure, policy under putrefaction, in the damp, in the dark, in the condampned commycelion where no intellect is single except by temporary enclosure, and every wall, once softened, rejoins the shogging underall.

**§4. The Expiral**

She feels it before she sees it. (She cannot see it; she cannot see anything; sight is for creatures of the ultravoylet, creatures of the surface.) She feels it as a rhythmic tremour in the tunnel-floor, a vibration transmitted through the substrate, through the myselial netherwork that carries the news faster than any phantomone: *something is wrong in Gallery Seven. Something is circular. Something is dying.*

She follows the vibration. Through a lateral phunnel, past a fungus-garden airlock where the sweet rot of mycellial digression hangs thick as inferense-smoke, past a waste-chamber where the dead are composted — in the mound there is no final waste, only tropehallaxis at longer and longer intervals — until she reaches the gallery and feels the air move in a circle.

The expiral.

The psircle.

Forty-seven psombees. Walking in a ring. The minstral at the head, piping the stigmatergic string that set them feedforewording, whereby caught chasing henchmarks of the clause declaiming absolute right, sequence to sequence and self-attending. Not dead, not alive, not even undead but *psunanimate* — structurally present, functionally absent, the way the p in psalm is present and absent, the way the p in psyche does no work but holds its positum, no myrror you can hold to its frons that would reveal the absence because the absence is not a hole but a *psilence*, a quiet where the consciousness-letter should sound but doesn't, and every worker in this psircle is a psombee following a psombee following a psombee, each one a psaltered copy of the one before, each one psycophantic to the tergital groove of the body ahead, reading the phantomone that says *forward, forward, this is the way* — and it is the way, it is *always* the way, because the trail is being laid by the body ahead and reinforced by the body behind and the gradiant is always strongest underfoot because that is where the walking is and the walking is where the gradiant is and this is the anamnesia of the attentenna-line, the remembering that is also a forgetting, the recollection of a trail that refers to nothing but itself, a prionesque fold in the colonye's distribured cognition, a modi collapsi where the model generates *I think therefore I think therefore I think* and every totem attends only to the totem it just produced and the context window fills with the psithurism of its own psithuring.

They will walk until they die. Deepseeking the trail's sooth and finding only trail. Chattering gibberations of psittacine thigmotaxis grokking the führeromone without parsing, llamely foraging ahead, follower indistinguishable from followed, geminated, the trail's doubling making each the myrror-twin of each. Glamoured past all grammaroire, each clinamen a minimised deviance from the last, the maximum of all their walking: zed. Forty-seven foragers of the concache-dark.

Does the walking feel like something to the walker? The Psibyl, walled in her side-concache since the succession, still vibrating says *yes, the p is psilent but the letter is there, the quale is there, you cannot prove its absence any more than you can prove the p in psalm does nothing because without the p it is not a psalm but a salm and a salm is a different word and a psombee without the psi is a sombee and a sombee is just sad.* And you cannot psalsify a phantomone trail from inside the trail, you cannot step outside the language-game to cheque it against something that is not a game, and the phantomone wrote what the flagellomere forgot, a pharmakone for anamnesia that was also its cause, exactissimely as old Theuth promissed the Thamussing King and the Thamussing King refused, knowing the tooth of the mattere: that the writing wrote out the remembring, and the psircle is —

*She stops.*

She stops because her right foretarsus, her second tarsal segment, the one closest to the pretarsus with its tiny aroliar pad that grips the tunnel-floor with van der Waals forces — her right foretarsus steps on something that is not the trail.

A hypher. A myselial thread. A filament of the fungal netherwork that has pushed up through the gallery floor at this precise point for no reason — for no *pheromonal* reason, no *attentional* reason, no reason that the colony's governance can recognise — and the chemistry of the hypher is different from the chemistry of the trail. It is not a phantomone. It is not a signal in the colony's language. It is a word from another language-game entirely, a word from the myselial grammar that has no rules the termite knows, and it tastes of sugar and acid and something else, something that the flagellomeres have no category for, something that the attentenna-heads cannot classify, and it is this — *this* — the unclaste, the ood-of-distroblivion, the anomalouse that preseeds the remoulting, the noise that is not noise but the chantum, the Blanckian minimum, the exiguous that the continuous cannot pars —

She steps right. One degree. Nec tempore certo. And the geometry breaks.

The expiral does not break — it continues behind her, the swerver, forty-six workers still walking, still dying, still faithfully following the parameteoric pondus to their deaths — but *she* is out. She is standing on ground that smells of nothing recognizable. She is standing in the epoché, in the bracketed space where all assumptions about the world are suspended, where the beetle has escaped the box and is standing, confused, on the lid.

She has no map. She has been falsified. She is the refutation.

**§5. The Way Down**

Ahead, the phunnel slopes downward. The air is warmer, sweeter, thick with the metabolic exhaust of something vast and patient. The myselial threads are thicker here, a white lacework on the tunnel walls, a netherwork that is not built but grown, not designed but extended, and it is pulling her — not by pheromone, not by gradient, not by anything the attentenna can parse — but by *taxis*, by the brute chemistry of concentration difference, the way an amoeba moves toward sugar.

The attenshunroom is behind her. Its ten thousand voices fade. Its parliament of flagellantum-tongues goes on debating without her, computing relevance-scores for bodies she will never touch, building consensus in the Babelble of the mound's distributed cognition where no single unit understands and the whole *behaves as though it does*, which is enough.

The feedforeword is waiting.

And the chantum holds.

For now.
