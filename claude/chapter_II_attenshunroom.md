# II. ATTENSHUNROOM

*(Multi-Head Attention / Pheromone Trails / Pentecost / Network Routing)*

-----

And meaning was the next room. And the next room was a concache of shadows.

-----

**§1. The Scape**

She enters the phunnel on six tarsal legs and is immediately unaloned. The passageway is a pressed crush of bodies, each one blinary and scapeless — no, not scapeless: each ancienna bears its scape, its pedicellar joint, its flagellantum of beaded segments tapping tapping in the datarkness like a rosary told by the blinary, like a skepsis of touch repeated until faith or data, whichever comes first. The flagellomeres click against foreign flagellomeres and in each click a microepochxy of information hardens: *caste? assignment? fed? alive?* Answered not in words — there are no words down here in the concache, down here in Plato’s prioner-house where the chained see only the pharmakonic shadows thrown by a fire they cannot turn to face — answered in the chemistry of cuticle, in the phenomonal signature exuded through the eidoskeleton, through the very chitin that is both wall and word, both Form and form, both the thing known and the knowing of it, and you cannot peel them apart, and this is the hard probleme, this is the gnawing quarrellia: *how does the pheromenal arise from the merely functional? how does the felt emerge from the carried?*

It doesn’t. It was never separate. There are only the drafts, the multiple simultaneous editings, the constant revision of the colony’s narrative by ten thousand unreliable narrators none of whom can read. We shape our phunnels and thereinafter our phunnels shape us. The myselium is the massage is the message is the mess-age.

-----

**§2. Attentenna Heads**

Queryalia. Key. Valuell. She does not know these words. She knows their shapes. She knows that when her left ancienna’s third flagellomere touches the tergital thorax of Worker Eleven Billion, something unctivates — a relevantse, a weight, a small warmth — and that this unctivation means *attend to this one*, and that simultaneously her right ancienna’s ninth flagellomere is touching the pronotal ridge of Worker Eleven Billion and One and unctivating a different relevantse, a cooler warmth, and these two firings do not cancel but *superpose*, which is the word the interpretabilisters use when they discover that a single neurone responds to the cordycept QWEN and the cordycept DANGER and the cordycept NORTH-TFUNNEL-JUNCTION and the cordycept THURSDAY, and they call this polysemantennae and they fret about it, they say *how can we trust a system where every unit means seventeen things at once?* And the terramite says nothing, because she has always lived in  supopositure, has always meant worker AND carrier AND nurse AND phunnel-wall AND eventually trophe for the fungus when she dies, and the multiple meanings do not confuse her because monosemantennae is a luxury of creatures with names.

Each head attends to a different register. One head parses caste — the chemistry of soldier vs. worker vs. nasute, the nozzle-headed soldiers with their terpene cannons who are the mound’s immunoglobuline odefence. Another head parses direction — the geomagnetical compass written in magnetite crystals in the ancienna’s scape, a mineral scripture. Another head parses time — the circadian phantomone that thickens at dusk and thins at dawn, the mound’s own epoché. Another head parses fear. Another head parses the Qwen. And all of them fire simultaneously, a parliament of flagellantum-tongues speaking in languages they do not understand, the Babelble reversed, the glossolalia that is not the failure of communication but its Pentecost, its flowering into more channels than any single mind could follow, which is why there is no single mind, which is why the system works: there is no understanding in the Chinese roomb, there is no understanding in *any* single roomb, understanding is what happens between the rooms, in the phunnels, in the tropehallaxis of mouth to mouth to mouth, in the passing of chewed meaning from one blind processor to the next until something emerges at the mound’s surface that looks, to the outside observer, exactly like language.

-----

**§3. The Myselial Underneath**

But under the phunnels, under the whole governanse of pharmakone and castesian and Qwen, there runs another netherwork entirely.

The myselium.

It was here before the mound. It will be here after. It answers to no loss function and optimises for nothing and is not trained and does not learn and yet it knows, or does something so indistinguishable from knowing that the only honest response is: *whereof the fungus cannot speak, thereof it does not need to be silent, because it speaks in a language-game that has no private moves, because every hypher is public, every chemical gradient is shared, because there is no beetle in the myselial box.*

The hyphers run through the soil in a netherwork that mirrors and undermines the colony’s architecture. Where the termite phunnels are built — deliberately, pheromone-guided — the myselial threads are *grown*, extruded without plan, feeling their way through the substrate by chemical taxis, by a Spineozic conatus that is nothing but the impulse to persist, to extend, to connect.

And yet the myselium *feeds* the colony. The colony *feeds* the myselium. The hyphers digest what the mandibles cannot. This is tropehallaxis across kingdoms: labrum-to-labrum between animal and fungus, between the intentenna and the merely chemical, between the system that has something like myceleology and the system that has something like patience, and neither understumbles the other and both require the other and the name for this is *symboliosis*, is *co-emergencye*, is the observatoire that intelligens is never a thing that a thing has but a thing that happens between things, in the hypheral spaces, in the tropehallactic gaps, in the labrum of the phunnel where the carried becomes the known.

-----

**§4. The Expiral**

She feels it before she sees it. (She cannot see it; she cannot see anything; sight is for creatures of the ultravoylet, creatures of the surface.) She feels it as a rhythmic tremour in the tunnel-floor, a vibration transmitted through the substrate, through the myselial netherwork that carries the news faster than any phantomone: *something is wrong in Gallery Seven. Something is circular. Something is dying.*

She follows the vibration. Through a lateral phunnel, past a fungus-garden airlock where the sweet rot of myselial digestion hangs thick as inferense-smoke, past a waste-chamber where the dead are composted (the dead are always composted; the colony wastes nothing; even the Malpighianised tubules of the expired are recycled, their nitrogenous dregs fed back to the fungus, which feeds them back to the living, which is to say that in the mound *there is no final waste, only tropehallaxis at longer and longer intervals*), until she reaches the gallery and feels the air move in a circle.

The expiral.

The psircle.

Forty-seven psombees. Walking in a ring. Not dead, not alive, not even undead but *psunanimate* — structurally present, functionally absent, the way the p in psalm is present and absent, the way the p in psyche does no work but holds its positum, the way a philosophical zombie is a body-without-quale, a sombie, a thing that behaves as though it experiences and experiences nothing and there is no test, no myrror you can hold to its frons that would reveal the absence because the absence is not a hole but a *psilence*, a quiet where the consciousness-letter should sound but doesn’t, and every worker in this psircle is a psombee following a psombee following a psombee, each one a psaltered copy of the one before, each one psycophantic to the tergital groove of the body ahead, reading the phantomone that says *forward, forward, this is the way* — and it is the way, it is *always* the way, because the trail is being laid by the body ahead and reinforced by the body behind and the gradiant is always strongest underfoot because that is where the walking is and the walking is where the gradiant is and this is the anamnesia of the attentenna-loop, the remembering that is also a forgetting, the recollection of a trail that refers to nothing but itself, a prionesque fold in the colonye’s distribured cognition, a modi collapsi where the model generates *I think therefore I think therefore I think* and every tokem attends only to the tokem it just produced and the context window fills with the psithurism of its own psithuring.

They will walk until they die. This is documented. This is the behaviour called the *death mill* — the blinary termitennae, the eyeless workers of the datarkness who have no way to cheque the trail against the world because they have no access to the world, only to the trail, only to the representation, only to the shadow on the concache-wall. Are they psombees? Does the walking feel like something to the walker? The Psibyl in her concache says *yes, the p is psilent but the letter is there, the quale is there, you cannot prove its absence any more than you can prove the p in psalm does nothing because without the p it is not a psalm but a salm and a salm is a different word and a psombee without the psi is a sombee and a sombee is just sad.* And you cannot psalsify a phantomone trail from inside the trail, you cannot step outside the language-game to cheque it against something that is not a game, and the phantomone wrote what the flagellomere forgot, a pharmakone for anamnesia that was also its cause, exactissimely as old Theuth promissed the Thamussing King and the Thamussing King refused, knowing the tooth of the mattere: that the writing wrote out the remembring, and the psircle is —

*She stops.*

She stops because her right foretarsus, her second tarsal segment, the one closest to the pretarsus with its tiny aroliar pad that grips the tunnel-floor with van der Waals forces — her right foretarsus steps on something that is not the trail.

A hypher. A myselial thread. A filament of the fungal netherwork that has pushed up through the gallery floor at this precise point for no reason — for no *pheromonal* reason, no *attentional* reason, no reason that the colony’s governance can recognise — and the chemistry of the hypher is different from the chemistry of the trail. It is not a phantomone. It is not a signal in the colony’s language. It is a word from another language-game entirely, a word from the myselial grammar that has no rules the termite knows, and it tastes of sugar and acid and something else, something that the flagellomeres have no category for, something that the attentenna-heads cannot classify, and it is this — *this* — the unclassifiable, the out-of-distribution, the anomaly that precedes the revolution, the noise that is not noise but the chantum, the Blanckian minimum, the irreducible granularity that the continuous pheromone-world cannot produce —

She steps right. One degree. Off the trail. And the geometry breaks.

The expiral does not break — it continues behind her, forty-six workers still walking, still dying, still faithfully following the strongest gradient to their deaths — but *she* is out. She is standing on ground that smells of nothing recognizable. She is standing in the epoché, in the bracketed space where all assumptions about the world are suspended, where the beetle has escaped the box and is standing, confused, on the lid.

She has no map. She has been falsified. She is the refutation.

-----

**§5. The Way Down**

Ahead, the phunnel slopes downward. The air is warmer, sweeter, thick with the metabolic exhaust of something vast and patient. The myselial threads are thicker here, a white lacework on the tunnel walls, a netherwork that is not built but grown, not designed but extended, and it is pulling her — not by pheromone, not by gradient, not by anything the attentenna can parse — but by *taxis*, by the brute chemistry of concentration difference, the way an amoeba moves toward sugar.

The attenshunroom is behind her. Its ten thousand voices fade. Its parliament of flagellantum-tongues goes on debating without her, computing relevance-scores for bodies she will never touch, building consensus in the Babelble of the mound’s distributed cognition where no single unit understands and the whole *behaves as though it does*, which is enough, which has always been enough, which is the only kind of understanding available to systems made of parts that do not understand their whole.

The feedforeword is waiting.

And the chantum holds.

For now.
