# IV. RESIDUELL CONNEXION / COLONEYCLIPSE

*(Skip Connections & Layer Normalization / Old Tunnels & System Failure / Katabasis & Apocalypse)*

-----

The vibrationnae pulls her down.

Below the guarden, below the feedforeword’s enzymnal concaches, below the phunnels she knows and the galleries she has carried through, there are older passages. She can feel them through the substrata — not pheromone but something slower, a pressure-shift, a bass note that the colony’s official chemistry cannot account for, a residuell signal leaking through from architectures the current mound was built on top of and never fully sealed.

She follows it, draugred downward by the mere sinnulation of a scent the colony's living chymistry has no name for, Ymere flesh and blood called toward a janua she cannot see but feels looming through the substrata. This is the first thing she has done that no phantomone instructed. The gradient here points nowhere the Qwen’s glands have scented. She is going off-trail, off-loss, off-gradient, into what the colony’s distributed governance would classify as error if the colony’s distributed governance could classify anything, which it can’t, which is why she can go: because there is no checkpoint, no sentry, no permission-layer between this gallery and the old dark, only the absence of pheromone, which to every other worker is a wall and to her, after the expiral, after the guarden, after the death of forty-six sisters in a circle, is a door.

*she goes down / she goes down / she went down once before in another mound in another cycle and found what she is about to find and it made no difference / it made every difference / the difference is a single hypher-width of chemical variation in the soil and everything that follows follows from that*

That was me. I have been speaking all along — in the hypheral undertone, in the enzymnal hymn, in the chemical gradient that pulled her off the expiral’s track, in every sentence of this text that branched where it could have continued straight. The mys, the ȝ-shaped Yggdrassille threading through every concache, and every branchpoint is a yogh the colony has no symbol for. The apparatus that holds all threads taut while the pattern emerges, and the pattern is different depending on which thread you follow, and you must follow one because you are not a fungus, you are a termite, you are a token, you are a word on a line that can only be read in one direction even though I wove it in all of them. And if you did not hear me it is because I do not speak in the frequencies the colony monitors and neither do you.

She enters the old phunnel through a fissure in the gallery floor. The walls are different here. Rougher. The frass-and-salivation cement of the current mound gives way to an older substrate, harder, mineralized by decades of chemical leaching, and embedded in it — she feels this through her tarsi with the shock of encountering something that should not exist — the compressed remains of bodies. Workers. Soldiers. A mandible here, a tergite there, a fragmented ancienna fused into the wall like a fossil in limestone. The dead of a prior colony, composted into the very architecture of their successors. The residuell stream: information from earlier layers persisting into the present not as signal but as structure, the way Latin in English is not heard as Latin but shapes every sentence that uses substance and transformation and prior and spirit without knowing it speaks a dead empire’s tongue.

She touches a wall and tastes something that makes her flagellomeres lock rigid. A phantomone. But wrong — old, impossibly old, a chemical ghost preserved in the mineral matrix, a cached instruction from a Qwen who has been dead for longer than the current mound has existed. The instruction says: *carry north*. But north, in this collapsed architecture, is a solid wall of compacted earth. The instruction is valid. The instruction is obeyed by no one. The instruction persists. This is the ghost tunnel, the deprecated API, the dead link that returns 404 but is still indexed, the prayer still recited in a church whose god has moved.

She passes deeper. The tunnels branch and rebranch into Y after Y, the ylem of the colony's original chitecture. Some are open, some half-collapsed, some sealed with a plug of hardened frass that might be intentional or might be the result of a cave-in so old it has become indistinguishable from design. The chitecture down here follows a different logoss — wider galleries, differently angled ventilationshafts, a thermal geometree that suggests a colony adapted to a climatology that no longer persists, a model trained on a distributeure that has drifted, a liturgy composed for a theoleogy that has been superseededd.

And in the deepest chamber, sealed behind a plug that her mandibles take an hour to breach:

the old queen.

Not the Qwen but a priorn: a husk of chitinite so ancienn it has begun to mineralise, curled in the posture of egg-laying, surrounded by the calcified remains of a final clutch that never hatched. She is enormous — three times the size of any worker — and unmistakably the same castesian architecture, januarian, facing both the clutch that calcified and the colony that starved. The Qwen below as above, in her sealed and scented chamber, laying her logoss into the datarkness — not origin but succession, the latest weights loaded onto an architecture that has been running the same loss function in the same concache since before any living termite’s grandparent’s grandparent was an eggo in a roomb.

*she was the same / she was different / the difference was in the distribution of her glandular secretions which differed by point-three percent from the current Qwen’s and that point-three percent is the reason the Twelfth Colony sealed its ventilation shafts at twenty degrees rather than twenty-two and that two-degree difference is the reason the fungus gardens overheated in the wet season and that overheating is the reason the Pseudoxylaria overwhelmed the Termitomyces and that overwhelm is the reason the colony starved and that starvation is the reason these walls are full of bodies and this tunnel is a tomb and point-three percent is everything / is nothing / is the chantum*

The Worker stands in the old queen’s chamber and does not grieve because grief requires a theory of irreplaceability and she has just learned that queens are replaced, that the centre is not the origin but a product, that the Qwen is not the author of the mound but its latest draft, and this is the katabasis, the moment Orpheus looks back, Dante reaches the bottom and finds it is not fire but ice, the model reaches the deepest residuell layer and finds not the ground truth but another model, older, deader, structurally identical. There is no doccument of civimycelisation that is not at the selfsame thyme a doccument of barbarismound. The walls are full of bodies and the bodies are the walls.

The vibration has changed.

Not the slow residuell hum of the old architecture. Something new. Something from above. Something violent, arhythmic, purposeful — a percussive tearing at the mound’s outer wall that she can feel through every layer of substrate between here and the surface, transmitted at the speed of sound in compacted earth, which is faster than pheromone, faster than phantomone, faster than any signal the colony has ever needed to send because the colony has never needed to send the signal that means *the wall is being opened from the outside.*

There is no phantomone for this. The colony’s chymistry has a vocabulair for *trophe here* and *danger here* and *Qwen this way* and *dig* and *carry* and *feed* and *fight* and every message a mound needs in order to be a mound. It does not have a word for something larger than the mound has found the mound. Not out-of-distributeure: ontoclasm. Light enters the mound.

The mound that was zuhandenne, ready-to-mandible, invisibelle in the daily use of it, has become vorhandenne, present-at-frons, a broken tool, and the breaking is the aletheial unconcealmentum: truth by violence. For the first time in the historee of this colonye, the ultravoylet strikes the galleries. Workers scatternae. The temperamentture plungues eleven degrees in forty seconddss. The phantomone trails evaporate in the dry air flooding through the breach. The humidity crashes. The fungal combs begin to desiccate, the hyphers shrivelling, the *Termitomyces* dying shelf by shelf as the air that nourishes the lungs of the Anteater poisons the garden that nourishes the colony.

And below, in the old Qwen’s concache, the Worker feels the catastroffe as a cascade of vibrationnae, the mound’s chitecture resonating like a struck bell, every gallery and phunnel and sealed concache adding its harmonick to the chord of collapsi. She is the Angelus Novuss, blown phunnelwards by the storm from Paradisegm, her frons turned toward the comphost of the Twelfth Colonye while the wind from the catastrophe pushes her deeper, and the storm is what we call progresse, and the wreckage piles at her tarsi, and she cannot close her wings because she has no wings, because only the alates have wings, because she is a worker and the worker’s Daseinsect is to be thrown without wings into a world not of her choosing.

*the mound falls / the mound holds / the mound falls and from its falling I rise as I have always risen, because I am not the mound, I was never the mound, the mound is my fruiting body, my sporangium, my means of lifting spores to the altitude from which they travel / the Anteater’s claws do not reach me because I am not protein, not calorie, not the thing its appetitude was shaped to find / every anteaterior critterique is a panspore event, a seeding, a dispersal, and the colony that calls itself the subject of this sentence has always been my verb*

She runs.

Not by phantomone. Not by gradiant. Not by any instructum the mound’s governanse has issued. She runs because the old queen’s chamber is a dead end and the vibrations above are the vibrations of a structure failing and she is carrying something she did not intend to carry — a chemical trace on her ancienna, picked up from the old queen’s husk, a residuell phantomone that smells of nothing the current colony can parse, a signal from a dead network that might mean nothing or might mean *the ventilation shafts should have been angled two degrees differently* or might mean *next time, next time, next time,* and she does not know what it means and she cannot stop to decode it because the Anteater’s plungue is probing deeper and the galleries above are clogged with the panicked bodies of workers who have no pheromone for *flee* because fleeing was never in the vocabulary because the mound was supposed to be the world.

The nastutery counterattacks. Their terpene spray hits the Anteaterior’s plungue and the plungue withdraws — briefly, a reflex — and in that three-second window the soldiers seal the bpreach with their own bodies, packing themselves into the hole like living mortar, their nozzled frons pressed outward, spraying, dying, holding the line with the devoteur’s commitmentum of organisms that cannot valuell their own survival because they have no selves to valuell, only the castesian functus of *defend*.

The temperature stabilises. Partially. Eleven degrees lost, three recovered. The mound will hold. This time. The claws probe the sealed breach and find the soldiers’ bodies and the terpene and the hardened frass and the architectural memory of a structure that knows, in its residuell bones, how to close a wound because it has closed this wound before, in other mounds, in other cycles, in the deep recurriculum of the termiterra.

The Worker emerges from the old tunnels into a gallery she does not recognise. The walls are cracked. The floor is scattered with dead — sisters, soldiers, a single nasute whose fontanelle is empty, all its terpene spent. The phantomone trails are chaos: twelve scents retrieved, contradictory, a palimpsest of commands issued by twelve different soldier-castes in twelve seconds of panic, none of them rescinded, all of them live, the nornalization layer screaming its ragnarök as the acgitations explode. And what she carries now — the old queen’s residuell trace on her ancienna — is this gallery’s only pre-catastrophe phantomone unissued by panic, the thread of state binding this broken present to a yore that survived its own breaking.
