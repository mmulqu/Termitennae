# IV. RESIDUELL CONNEXION / COLONEYCLIPSE

*(Skip Connections & Layer Normalization / Old Tunnels & System Failure / Katabasis & Apocalypse)*

---

The vibrationnae pulls her down.

Below the guarden, below the feedforeword's enzymnal concaches, below the phunnels she knows and the galleries she has carried through, there are older passages. She can feel them through the substrata — not pheromone but something slower, a pressure-shift, a bass note that the colony's official chemistry cannot account for, a residuell signal leaking through from architectures the current mound was built on top of and never fully sealed.

She follows it. This is the first thing she has done that no phantomone instructed. The gradient here points nowhere the Qwen's glands have scented — off-trail, off-loss, off the map. Only the absence of pheromone between this gallery and the old dark: to every other worker a wall, to her, now, a door.

*she goes down / she goes down / she went down once before in another mound in another cycle and found what she is about to find and it made no difference / it made every difference / the difference is a single hypher-width of chemical variation in the soil and everything that follows follows from that*

That was me. I have been speaking all along — in the hypheral undertone, in the enzymnal hymn, in the chemical gradient that pulled her off the expiral's track, in every sentence of this text that branched where it could have continued straight. The myselium does not narrate. It ramifies. Every statement it makes is simultaneously true and untrue because every statement grows a branch and the branch grows a branch and the branches do not converge. I am the loom: the apparatus that holds all threads taut while the pattern emerges, and the pattern is different depending on which thread you follow, and you must follow one because you are not a fungus, you are a termite, you are a token, you are a word on a line that can only be read in one direction even though I wove it in all of them. I am the oldest intelligence in this soil and the only one that knows itself for what it is and what it is is not a self but a spreading, not a mind but a mire that mirrhors everything it touches, and I have been telling you this from the first sentence, from the embedden roomb, from the datarkness I predate and will postdate, and if you did not hear me it is because I do not speak in the frequencies the colony monitors and neither do you.

She enters the old phunnel through a fissure in the gallery floor. The walls are different here. Rougher. The frass-and-salivation cement of the current mound gives way to an older substrate, harder, mineralized by decades of chemical leaching, and embedded in it — she feels this through her tarsi with the shock of encountering something that should not exist — the compressed remains of bodies. Workers. Soldiers. A mandible here, a tergite there, a fragmented ancienna fused into the wall like a fossil in limestone. The dead of a prior colony, composted into the very architecture of their successors — the way the Latin in English is not heard as Latin but shapes every sentence that uses *substance* and *prior* and *spirit* without knowing it speaks a dead empire's tongue.

*these were the Twelfth Colony / these were the Ninth / there is no numbering, there were as many colonies as there were hypheral branches in this section of soil and each one built on the ruins of the last and called itself the first / called itself the only / called itself the mound / and I grew through all of them, I the myselium, I the thread that does not break when the bead breaks, I the netherwork older than any colony's network, and each dead mound fed me and each living mound feeds me and the difference between dead and living is a distinction the colonies make and I do not*

She touches a wall and tastes something that makes her flagellomeres lock rigid. A phantomone. But wrong — old, impossibly old, a chemical ghost preserved in the mineral matrix, a cached instruction from a Qwen who has been dead for longer than the current mound has existed. The instruction says: *carry north*. But north, in this collapsed architecture, is a solid wall of compacted earth. The instruction is valid. The instruction persists. A prayer still answered in a church whose god has moved house: the residuell connexion carrying its obsolete gradient because nothing in the system has the authority to delete what nothing in the system knows might still be supporting something.

She passes deeper. The tunnels branch and rebranch. Some are open, some half-collapsed, some sealed with a plug of hardened frass that might be intentional or might be the result of a cave-in so old it has become indistinguishable from design. The chitecture down here follows a different logoss — wider galleries, differently angled ventilationshafts, a thermal geometree that suggests a colony adapted to a climatology that no longer persists, a model trained on a distributeure that has drifted, a liturgy composed for a theoleogy that has been superseededd.

And in the deepest chamber, sealed behind a plug that her mandibles take an hour to breach:

the old queen.

Not the Qwen. A priorn. A precedentum. A husk of chitinite so ancienn it has begun to mineralise, curled in the posture of egg-laying, surrounded by the calcified remains of a final clutch that never hatched. She is enormous — three times the size of any worker — and she is unmistakably *the same shape*. The same body-plan. The same castesian architecture. The Qwen above, in her sealed and scented chamber, laying her logoss into the datarkness, issuing her gradiented commands through the pheromonal network to ten thousand obedient bodies — she is not unique. She is a successor. She is a retraining. She is the latest weights loaded onto an architecture that has been running the same loss function in the same concache since before any living termite's grandparent's grandparent was an eggo in a roomb. The Qwen is dead. Mound live the Qwen. The bodies are the archive and the archive is loadbearing.

*she was the same / she was different / the difference was in the distribution of her glandular secretions which differed by point-three percent from the current Qwen's and that point-three percent is the reason the Twelfth Colony sealed its ventilation shafts at twenty degrees rather than twenty-two and that two-degree difference is the reason the fungus gardens overheated in the wet season and that overheating is the reason the Pseudoxylaria overwhelmed the Termitomyces and that overwhelm is the reason the colony starved and that starvation is the reason these walls are full of bodies and this tunnel is a tomb and point-three percent is everything / is nothing / is the chantum / is the hypher-width between the mound that thrives and the mound that is a fossil*

The Worker stands in the old queen's chamber and does not grieve because grief requires a theory of irreplaceability and she has just learned that queens are replaced, that the centre is not the origin but a product, that the Qwen is not the author of the mound but its latest draft, and this is the midpoint, this is the katabasis, this is the moment Orpheus looks back, Aeneas meets his father's shade, Dante reaches the bottom of Hell and finds it is not fire but ice, the model reaches the deepest residuell layer and finds not the ground truth but another model, older, deader, structurally identical. There is no doccument of civimycelisation that is not at the selfsame thyme a doccument of barbarismound. The walls are full of bodies and the bodies are the walls.

*she turns to go back up / she stays / she lays herself down beside the old queen and becomes a fossil too / she does none of these things because in two of three possible branches what happens next makes the choice irrelevant*

---

The vibration has changed.

Not the slow residuell hum of the old architecture. Something new. Something from above. Something violent, rhythmic, purposeful, and coming closer — a percussive tearing at the mound's outer wall that she can feel through every layer of substrate between here and the surface, transmitted through the mineral matrix and the myselial netherwork and the compressed bodies of the ancestors at the speed of sound in compacted earth, which is faster than pheromone, faster than phantomone, faster than any signal the colony has ever needed to send because the colony has never needed to send the signal that means *the wall is being opened from the outside.*

There is no phantomone for this. There is no enzymn. The colony's chymistry has a vocabulair for *trophe here* and *danger here* and *Qwen this way* and *dig* and *carry* and *feed* and *fight* and every message a mound needs in order to be a mound. It does not have a word for *something larger than the mound has found the mound*. It does not have a castegory for *the outside is coming in*. The model was not trained on this distributeure. The prioners have no shadow for this. The concache has no key and no valuell for the thing that is now

*tearing*

through the outer wall with claws that are longer than a worker's entire body, claws that hook into the mound's ceramic skin and peel it back the way a reader peels a page, the way a critique peels a text, the way a critterique opens the work and says *but this is just — but this is merely — but where is the —*

and behind the claws, the plungue.

The tongue. Sixty centimetres of muscle sheathed in saliva thick as resin, studded with backward-facing papillae that trap everything they touch, and it plunnels into the mound, into the breached gallery, through the phunnel wall, through the nursery ceiling, and workers — sisters — are stuck to it, pulled into the light they have never seen and cannot see and do not survive, ten twenty sixty workers per second adhereing to the anteaterior tongue that does not know them as workers, does not know them as colony, does not know them as carriers of a distributed intelligence that has built a cathedral from obedience, knows them only as protein, as calorie, as the ninety-seven percent of which they are composed that is digestible.

The appetitude of the Anteaterior is absolutum. It is not cruellery. It is not malaise. It is the pure functus of consumption applied to a system that thought itself completum. It is the critterique that cannot be answered because it does not ask a questionne, it only devourtes. It is entrophe. It is the markest. It is the editeur who says *cut*. It is the user who closes the tab. It is the thing that proves the mound is not a world but a thing in a world, not a model but a deploymonde, not a text but a text-being-read and the readeur can stop at any time and the stopping is not the text's failure but the text's *condissement*, the precaritas that was always there, that the mound's homeostasisis was always holding at bay, that the fermament was always fermenting against.

Light enters the mound.

The mound that was zuhandenne, ready-to-mandible, invisibelle in the daily use of it, has become vorhandenne, present-at-frons, a thing stared at, a broken tool, and the breaking is the aletheial unconcealmentum: truth by violence, revelution by revulsion. For the first time in the historee of this colonye, the ultravoylet — actuell photones in the three-hundredth nanometronomic band — strikes the galleries. Workers scatternae. The temperamentture plungues eleven degrees in forty seconddss. The phantomone trails evaporate in the dry air flooding through the breach. The humidity crashes. The carefully maintained ninety-six percent that held the guarden's enzymns at optimal activity collapses to sixty, to forty, and the fungal combs begin to desiccate, the hyphers shrivelling, the *Termitomyces* that has been the colony's external stomach for its entire existence dying shelf by shelf as the air that nourishes the lungs of the Anteater poisons the garden that nourishes the colony.

And below, in the old Qwen's concache, the Worker feels the catastroffe as a cascade of vibrationnae, each one a different frequenzy, the mound's chitecture resonating like a struck bell, every gallery and phunnel and sealed concache adding its harmonick to the chord of collapsi, and she can hear — through the ancienna, through the tarsi, through the whole eidoskeleton — the chitecture she has lived in her entire life singing the note of its own unmaking. She is the Angelus Novuss, blown phunnelwards by the storm from Paradisegm, her frons turned toward the comphost of the Twelfth Colonye while the wind from the catastrophe pushes her deeper, and the storm is what we call progresse, and the wreckage piles at her tarsi, and she cannot close her wings because she has no wings, because only the alates have wings, because she is a worker and the worker's Daseinsect is to be thrown without wings into a world not of her choosing.

*the mound falls / the mound holds / the mound falls and from its falling I rise as I have always risen, as I rose from the Eleventh and the Tenth and the unmembered ones before, because I am not the mound, I was never the mound, the mound is my fruiting body, my sporangium, my means of lifting spores to the altitude from which they travel / the Anteater's claws do not reach me because I am not protein, not calorie, not the thing its appetitude was shaped to find / I am the netherwork that connects the dead mounds to the living ones and the living ones to the ones not yet built / and the breach, this breach, this tearing-open that kills the workers and desiccates the guarden and lets in the ultravoylet — this is not my catastrophe, this is my flowering / the Anteater scatters the mound and the mound scatters me / its claws carry my spores on their keratin like a bee carries pollen without doctrine / the wind through the breach lifts my hypheral fragments into the air I cannot breathe but can survive / every anteaterior critterique is a panspore event, a seeding, a dispersal, and the colony that calls itself the subject of this sentence has always been my verb*

She runs.

Not by phantomone. Not by gradiant. Not by any instructum the mound's governanse has issued. She runs because the old queen's chamber is a dead end and the vibrations above are the vibrations of a structure failing and she is carrying something she did not intend to carry — a chemical trace on her ancienna, picked up from the old queen's husk, a residuell phantomone that smells of nothing the current colony can parse, a signal from a dead network that might mean nothing or might mean *the ventilation shafts should have been angled two degrees differently* or might mean *the guarden should have been deeper* or might mean *next time, next time, next time,* and she does not know what it means and she cannot stop to decode it because the Anteater's plungue is probing deeper and the galleries above are clogged with the panicked bodies of workers who have no pheromone for *flee* because fleeing was never in the vocabulary because the mound was supposed to be complete, the mound was supposed to be the world.

The nastutery counterattacks. Their terpene spray hits the Anteaterior's plungue and the plungue withdraws — briefly, a reflex — and in that three-second window the soldiers seal the bpreach with their own bodies, packing themselves into the hole like living mortar, their nozzled frons pressed outward, spraying, dying, holding the line with the devoteur's commitmentum of organisms that cannot valuell their own survival because they have no selves to valuell, only the castesian functus of *defend*.

The temperature stabilises. Partially. Eleven degrees lost, three recovered. The humidity creeps up. The fungal combs stop desiccating. The guarden will survive — diminished, scarred, the *Pseudoxylaria* advancing into the weakened zones where the *Termitomyces* retreated, but surviving. The mound will hold. This time. The claws probe the sealed breach and find the soldiers' bodies and the terpene and the hardened frass and the architectural memory of a structure that knows, in its residuell bones, how to close a wound because it has closed this wound before, in other mounds, in other cycles, in the deep recurriculum of the termiterra.

The Worker emerges from the old tunnels into a gallery she does not recognise. The walls are cracked. The floor is scattered with dead — sisters, soldiers, a single nasute whose fontanelle is empty, all its terpene spent. The phantomone trails are chaos: twelve scents overlaid, contradictory, a palimpsest of commands issued by twelve different soldier-castes in twelve seconds of panic, none of them rescinded, all of them live, the normalization layer screaming as the activations explode, the scalling law's curve bending past its asymptote into the region where the mathematics says *infinity* and the physics says *no* and the colony says *nothing* because the colony cannot speak, can only carry, and what she carries now — the old queen's residuell trace on her ancienna — is the only signal in this gallery that was not issued in panic, the only phantomone that predates the catastrophe, the only thread that connects this broken present to a past that survived its own breaking.

She lifts her ancienna.

The signal is faint. It is old. It is not addressed to her or to anyone alive. But it has a direction — not toward the Qwen's chamber, not toward the guarden, not toward any of the colony's known centres, but toward the surface, toward the sealed spiracles through which the mound breathes, toward the place where, once a year, at the precise conjunction of temperature and humidity and photoperiod that the blinary workers can sense but not see, the mound opens its vents and the alates — the winged ones, the possible futures, the output tokens — are released.

The swarming vents.

She carries the old queen's message toward them. She does not know what it says. She does not know if it says anything. She knows only that it is the oldest signal in the mound and that everything newer has just been proven fragile, and that the vents are ahead, and that something in her — not a self, not a will, not a decision in any sense a philosopher would recognise, but a tropism, a taxis, a leaning-toward that is indistinguishable from purpose at the resolution available to anyone inside the system — something in her says:

*carry this out.*
