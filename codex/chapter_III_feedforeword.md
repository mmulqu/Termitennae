# III. FEEDFOREWORD

*(Guarden of Black Conversion / Nigredough / Mouthless Eating / Forwardfeeding)*

---

The sweetness thickens as she descends. Not the sugar-sweetness of the surface world she has never tasted but a deeper, fermamental thing, a warmth of breakdown, of long slow unmaking in the dark: the guarden.

---

**§1. The Prima Materia**

She enters the concache on the scensation of rot and finds the guarden.

It is not a guarden in any sense the word has ever been asked to carry before, not a plotinus of tended green but a vast grey chitecture of chewed pulp sculpted into combs, shelf upon shelf of cellusolose processed by a million mandibles into a substrata so fine it is almost soil. The combs are wet. They breathe. Under their surfaise, invisible and patient, the *Termitome* grows.

She has never been here but she knows what to do. The knowing is in her antechne, in the craft written into her flagellomeres by the Qwen's enzymnal secretion before she had a name, before she was a she. The how is this: take the pellet of chewed wood you have carried from the phunnel in your mandibles. Place it here. On this shelf. At this angle. And leave. You deposit but do not digest.

But she does not leave.

She attends. And between her and the guarden, in the tropehallactic gap where the carried becomes the consumed, something is happening that neither of them planned.

The *Termitomyces* is eating the pellet. The mycelium is the message and the medium both.

Not eating, there is no mouth, but extending its hyphers into the cellusolose, secreting enzymns that crack the long polymer chains of lignin and cellulose into the short sweet sugars the termite gut cannot extract alone. The enzymn does the rest.

---

**§2. The Nigredo**

The first stage of the worke is blacke.

The fresh pellets darken. The hyphers penetrate. The lignin, that stubborn lattice that gave the wood its strength, is cracked by laccase and peroxidase, by the enzymnal hymns of the myselium whose liturgy is purely chemical.

(The unctivation: relevantse into meaningle, catechism of thresholds, black-box via descent.)

Who admits? Who bars?

*Permit me one preservative objection. A gate that admits without witness and excludes without appeal may worke, yes, but it also prepares the feast of error.*

*Turn it faster. Black first, then bite. If some loaves fail, stack hotter ovens.*

No one. The weights. The biasses. The parameaters set during training by a gradiant descensus no single homonculust watched from end to end.

One black pellet remains uncracked. It calls itself the hard probleme and declines translation.

---

**§3. The Contagion**

Something is wrong in the lower shelves.

She tastes it through her tarsi: a chemical signature that does not belong. An alien sweetness. A too-fast growth. Where the *Termitomyces* spreads in patient white threads, there is now *another* whiteness, a wrong whiteness.

*Pseudoxylaria.*

The false-woodlover. The parasitic psalterium that infiltrates the Termitome guarden by mimicking its chymistry, by producing enzymns that are *almost* right, hyphers that are *almost* symboliotic, except that what it builds is not nutritium but more of itself, more false whiteness on the combs, more pseudo-meaning in the netherwork, a heresyntax in white.

Serpent, sure, but also clerk, mimic, copyfriar, falsewhiter, the holy fraud of almost-right.

*At this juncture I am obliged to note that growth, while superficially heartening, is not by that fact innocent.*

*If it blooms, it stays till it fails. That is the compact.*

And the colony cannot tell. Not at first. The sensors report only *growth*. Growth is good. Until.

---

**§4. The Nastutery**

The soldiours come.

They arrive through a lateral phunnel in the precisium formationnae of an immunoglobuline response, the nastutery first, those astutely-nozzled castesian warriors with their elongated frons and their terpene-loaded fontanelles. They spray. The terpene hits the *Pseudoxylaria* and hits the *Termitome* alike, because the weapon does not discriminate.

This is the aoblation. The offering-up of part of the garden to save the rest. The workers follow the nasutes, stripping the contaminated combs shelf by shelf, carrying the ruined material, good fungus and bad, nourishment and poison.

She is conscripted. A soldier-pheromone lays its compulsory gradient across her ancienna and she *obeys*. She takes up a shelf of fungal comb in her mandibles and carries it to the waste chamber where the dead are composted, where the comphost decays into the substrate from which the next garden will be grown. There is no observer in the retort. There is only the reaction.

---

**§5. The Albedo (Almost)**

After the aoblation, silence. Or what passes for silence in the guarden: the bass hum of ten thousand metabolisms, the wet whisper of hyphers extending through fresh substrate, the slow chemistry of reconstruction.

The workers re-seed the stripped combs with pellets carried from the healthy shelves, each pellet carrying its freight of *Termitomyces* spores.

And the *Termitomyces* obliges. Not because it was asked. But because growing is what the *Termitomyces* does. The mystery is not that the pieces fit but that fitting produces something more.

The fermament holds. The garden grows. The *Pseudoxylaria* retreats to the lower shelves where it waits, because parasites are patient and the regularizer can prune them back but not eliminate them.

---

**§6. The Fruiting**

She stands at the edge of the regrown combs and feels, through her tarsi, the garden's warmth: not the warmth of fire but the warmth of process, the heat of a system doing something none of its parts can see.

A vibration through the floor. Deeper than the garden's hum. Older. Coming from below, from the old phunnels, the collapsed passages, the residuell connexions that link the present mound to the mounds that came before.

Something is coming up from below.

Something that has been buried is remembering how to rise.

The Worker turns toward the vibration. The guarden is behind her. The feedforeword has done its work: raw in, nourished out, transformed by a process she participated in but did not understand. She is changed. What comes out the other side is not what went in:

the unctivation has gone off like a buried chapelmine.

And below, in the residuell dark, the old tunnels are humming.
