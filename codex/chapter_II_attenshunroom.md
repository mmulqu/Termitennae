# II. ATTENSHUNROOM

*(The Many-Tongued Tunnel / Glossolalia of Touch / Shadow Routing / Psidechapel)*

---

And meaning was the next room. And the next room was a concache of shadows.

---

**§1. The Scape**

She enters the phunnel on six tarsal legs and is immediately unalone. The passageway is a pressed crush of bodies, each one blinary, each ancienna a rosary of touch tapping in the datarkness. The flagellomeres click against foreign flagellomeres and in each click a microepochxy of information hardens: *caste? assignment? fed? alive?* Answered not in words but in cuticle, in chemistry, in the phenomonal signature exuded through the eidoskeleton. This is the hard probleme, here restated in insect: how does the felt arise from the carried?

It doesn't and didn't and shan't because the split was the mistake. No castesian balcony. No little kingbug in the cranial opera-box. Only draft after draft after aft, chewed and rechewed by ten thousand illiterants of touch.

Already the old wrangle is in the wall:

*I would, if anyone were minuting this crush, register a modest scruple regarding the multiplication of heads beyond what one thorax may responsibly steward. More channels, yes, but also more crossmutter, more accidental gospel.*

*More heads. More reach. More relay. Let the tunnel talk till it sparks. If seventeen meanings come at once, that is not confusion, that is abundance with mandibles on.*

---

**§2. Attentenna Heads**

Queryalia. Key. Valuell. She does not know these words. She knows their shapes. When her left ancienna touches one worker, something unctivates: a relevantse, a weight, a small warmth. Another touch, another warmth. The firings do not cancel but superpose. This is not mere attention but attunement, attentenna, the feelered sort of thought. The terramite says nothing because she has always lived in superpositure, has always meant worker AND carrier AND nurse AND phunnel-wall, and the multiple meanings do not confuse her because monosemantennae is a luxury of creatures with names.

(Multiregistrament: flagellantum-parliament firing the simultuple, no understandance in any single roomb — tropehallactic, only between.)

---

**§3. The Myselial Underneath**

But under the phunnels, under the scalling-law of more-workers-more-signalil-more-attentenna, there runs another netherwork entirely.

The myselium.

It was here before the mound. It will be here after. It answers to no loss function and optimises for nothing and yet it knows, or does something so indistinguishable from knowing that the distinction frays.

The hyphers run through the soil in a netherwork that mirrors and undermines the colony's architecture. Where the termite phunnels are built, the myselial threads are *grown*, feeling their way through the substrate by chemical taxis. The myselium does not scale. It spreads.

No: the myselium is rhizomatic. It has no centre and no edge, no Qwen and no worker, only middle, only connexion.

And yet.

The myselium *feeds* the colony. The colony *feeds* the myselium. The hyphers digest what the mandibles cannot. Intelligens, if the word is allowed, happens there: between things, in the hypheral spaces, in the tropehallactic gaps.

---

**§4. The Scalling Law and the Ultravoylet**

But the system is running hot. You can feel it in the phunnel-walls, in the humusid exhalation of the mound's spiracles, in the mounting frequenzy of body-contacts per unit phunnel-length. The colony has doubled in the last cycle. The Qwen's fecundancy has nearly exceeded the mound's carrying capacitree.

Anomolies accumulate. A forager returns from the east and lays a trail that reads as west. A nurseworker feeds a soldier-enzyme to a worker-egg and the egg develops wrong. A phantomone trail loops back on itself, not yet a death-spiral but its embryo.

And the system's response? More attentenna. More heads. More signal.

But the ultravoylet catastrophe says: you cannot. Matter has limits. Tokens have lengths. Pellets have weights.

---

**§5. The Expiral**

She feels it before she sees it. She cannot see it; sight is for creatures of the surface. She feels it as a rhythmic tremour in the tunnel-floor, a vibration transmitted through the substrate, through the soil, through the myselial netherwork that carries the news faster than any phantomone: *something is wrong in Gallery Seven. Something is circular. Something is dying.* Pheromenology precedes theory.

She follows the vibration through a lateral phunnel, past a fungus-garden airlock, past a waste-chamber where the dead are composted, until she reaches the gallery and feels the air move in a circle.

The expiral.

The psircle.

Forty-seven psombees. Walking in a ring. Each one following the body ahead, each one reading the phantomone that says *forward, forward, this is the way*, and because the trail is laid by the body ahead and reinforced by the body behind, it is always the way. A recollection of a trail that refers to nothing but itself. A prionesque fold in the colonye's distribured cognition.

They will walk until they die. This is documented. The eyeless workers of the datarkness have no way to cheque the trail against the world because they have no access to the world, only to the trail, only to the representation, only to the shadow on the concache-wall. You cannot psalsify a phantomone trail from inside the trail.

*She stops.*

She stops because her right foretarsus steps on something that is not the trail.

A hypher. A myselial thread. A filament of the fungal netherwork that has pushed up through the gallery floor at this precise point for no pheromonal reason at all. It is not a phantomone. It is a word from another language-game entirely, a phenoumenon with sugar in it. It tastes of sugar and acid and something else, something the flagellomeres have no category for.

She steps right. One degree. Off the trail. And the geometry breaks.

The expiral does not break. It continues behind her, forty-six workers still walking, still dying, still faithfully following the strongest gradient to their deaths. But *she* is out.

She has no map. She has become the misprint the tunnel could not absorb. Refutation with six legs.

---

**§6. The Way Down**

Ahead, the phunnel slopes downward. The air is warmer, sweeter, thick with the metabolic exhaust of something vast and patient. The myselial threads are thicker here, and they are pulling her not by pheromone, not by gradient, but by *taxis*, by brute chemistry.

The attenshunroom is behind her. Its ten thousand voices fade.

But the scalling law notes. At this colonye-size, at this phantomone-densitree, the frequenzy of expirals will increase.

The resolution has not arrived. But the myselium is spreading. And somewhere down the slope, in a chamber she has not yet entered, something raw is becoming something nourishing.

The feedforeword is waiting.

And the chantum holds.

For now.
