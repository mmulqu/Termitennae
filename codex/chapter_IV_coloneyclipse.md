# IV. RESIDUELL CONNEXION / COLONEYCLIPSE

*(Old Bone Tunnels / Breachsong / Katabasis of the Next Time / Collapse Canticle)*

---

The vibrationnae pulls her down.

Below the guarden, below the feedforeword's enzymnal concaches, below the phunnels she knows, there are older passages. She can feel them through the substrata, not pheromone but something slower, a pressure-shift, a bass note that the colony's official chemistry cannot account for.

She follows it. This is the first thing she has done that no phantomone instructed. The gradient here points nowhere the Qwen's glands have scented. To every other worker the absence of pheromone is a wall. To her, after the expiral, it is a door.

*she goes down / she went down once before in another mound in another cycle / the difference is a single hypher-width of chemical variation in the soil and everything that follows follows from that*

That was me. I have been speaking all along, in the hypheral undertone, in the enzymnal hymn, in the chemical gradient that pulled her off the expiral's track. The myselium does not narrate. It ramifies. I am the oldest intelligence in this soil and what I am is not a self but a spreading, not a mind but a mire that mirrhors everything it touches.

---

She enters the old phunnel through a fissure in the gallery floor. The walls are different here. Rougher. The frass-and-salivation cement of the current mound gives way to an older substrate, harder, mineralized by decades of chemical leaching, and embedded in it the compressed remains of bodies. Workers. Soldiers. A mandible here, a tergite there. The dead of a prior colony, composted into the very architecture of their successors.

*these were the Twelfth Colony / these were the Ninth / there is no numbering / each one built on the ruins of the last and called itself the first / and I grew through all of them*

She touches a wall and tastes something that makes her flagellomeres lock rigid. A phantomone. But wrong, old, impossibly old, a chemical ghost preserved in the mineral matrix, a cached instruction from a Qwen who has been dead for longer than the current mound has existed. The instruction says: *carry north*. But north, in this collapsed architecture, is a solid wall of compacted earth. The instruction is valid. The instruction is obeyed by no one. The instruction persists, an old commandment salted in the loadbearing lime.

She passes deeper. The tunnels branch and rebranch. Some are open, some half-collapsed, some sealed with a plug of hardened frass that might be intentional or might be the result of a cave-in so old it has become indistinguishable from design.

And in the deepest chamber, sealed behind a plug that her mandibles take an hour to breach:

the old queen.

Not the Qwen. A priorn. A precedentum. A husk of chitinite so ancienn it has begun to mineralise, curled in the posture of egg-laying, surrounded by the calcified remains of a final clutch that never hatched. She is unmistakably *the same shape*.

*she was the same / she was different / and point-three percent is everything / is nothing / is the hypher-width between the mound that thrives and the mound that is a fossil*

The Worker stands in the old queen's chamber and learns the centre is a rerun with glands. Not origin but latest costume. Not motherfirst but motheragain. The queen is dead, mound live the sameagain. The bodies are the archive and the archive is loadbearing.

---

The vibration has changed.

Not the slow residuell hum of the old architecture. Something new. Something from above. Something violent, rhythmic, purposeful, and coming closer: a percussive tearing at the mound's outer wall.

There is no phantomone for this. The colony's chymistry has a vocabulair for *trophe here* and *danger here* and *Qwen this way* and *dig* and *carry* and *fight*. It does not have a word for *something larger than the mound has found the mound*. Of the outside it has no article, creed, nor carrying-clause at all.

*tearing*

through the outer wall with claws that are longer than a worker's entire body,

and behind the claws, the plungue.

The tongue. Sixty centimetres of muscle sheathed in saliva thick as resin, studded with backward-facing papillae that trap everything they touch, and it plunnels into the mound, into the breached gallery, through the phunnel wall, through the nursery ceiling, and workers are stuck to it, pulled into the light they have never seen and cannot survive.

The appetitude of the Anteaterior is edit without argument. Not evil. Not anger. The claw revises. The tongue enters already convinced. In that cut the mound discovers it was never world, only walled.

*You see, says the old brake-hidden voice, this is what was meant by reserve, by margin, by not spending the whole future in one season of laying.*

*Patch and proceed, says the other. Seal with bodies. Learn later.*

For the first time in the historee of this colonye, the ultravoylet strikes the galleries. Workers scatternae. The temperamentture plungues eleven degrees in forty seconddss. The phantomone trails evaporate in the dry air flooding through the breach. The humidity crashes. The carefully maintained ninety-six percent that held the guarden's enzymns at optimal activity collapses, and the fungal combs begin to desiccate.

*the mound falls / the mound holds / and from its falling I rise as I have always risen / because I am not the mound, the mound is my fruiting body / this is not my catastrophe, this is my flowering / the Anteater scatters the mound and the mound scatters me*

She runs.

Not by phantomone. Not by gradiant. She runs because the old queen's chamber is a dead end and the vibrations above are the vibrations of a structure failing and she is carrying something she did not intend to carry: a chemical trace on her ancienna, picked up from the old queen's husk, a residuell phantomone that smells of nothing the current colony can parse, a signal from a dead network that might mean *next time*.

The nastutery counterattacks. Their terpene spray hits the Anteaterior's plungue and the plungue withdraws, briefly, and in that three-second window the soldiers seal the breach with their own bodies, packing themselves into the hole like living mortar.

The temperature stabilises. Partially. The guarden will survive, diminished, scarred, but surviving. The mound will hold. This time.

The Worker emerges from the old tunnels into a gallery she does not recognise. The walls are cracked. The floor is scattered with dead. The phantomone trails are chaos: twelve scents overlaid, contradictory, none of them rescinded, all of them live. What she carries now, the old queen's residuell trace on her ancienna, is the only signal in this gallery that was not issued in panic.

She lifts her ancienna.

The signal is faint. It is old. It is not addressed to her or to anyone alive. But it has a direction, not toward the Qwen's chamber, not toward the guarden, but toward the surface, toward the swarming vents.

The swarming vents.

She carries the old queen's message toward them. She does not know what it says. She knows only that it is the oldest signal in the mound and that everything newer has just been proven fragile.

*carry this out.*
