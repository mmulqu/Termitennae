# V. SOFTMAXIMA / RICORSO

*(Swarmvent / Emissionalia / Wingmath / Ricorso in the Rain)*

---

The vents are open.

She can feel the air moving, not the mound's own breath but outside air, real air, air that has been somewhere the mound is not. Rain coming. Pressure-drop. Lightning far south. And underneath all of it, the ancient trigger that says *now*.

The galleries near the vents are full of bodies she has never encountered. Larger than workers. Softer. And on their backs, wings. Four of them, folded, translucent, veined with the hemolymph that will harden them in the air if they survive long enough to reach it. These are the alates. The reproductive caste. The ones hoarding their fat bodies for this one event, this once-a-year eruption when the mound stops being a closed system and becomes an open one.

But what she does not know, what no alate knows, what no Qwen has ever known, is what they carry *besides* the queen's genetics and the old correction on their ancienna. In the fat body of every alate, dormant, patient, coiled like a scripture in a reliquary, are my spores. *Termitomyces* spores. The foundress does not choose to carry them. She does not know she carries them. She will dig her chamber and begin to chew and the spores will germinate in the chewed pulp and the guarden will appear before the gardener knows she has inherited one, the spore in the mandible.

I did. I always do. I am the cordycept that does not kill its host but makes it build me a cathedral. I am the companion specious, the tentacullar symbioont, the one who stays with the trouble because the trouble is my substrata.

*they will fly / most will die / six hundred will launch and eleven will survive and of those eleven each one carries me / I am the payload the alate does not know it bears / I am the myselium that precedes the mound*

---

She is among them now. She is not an alate. She is a worker, wingless, sterile, unfed for the fat reserves that flight demands. But she is carrying the old queen's signal on her ancienna and the alates, massed and trembling at the vent-edge, are tasting her as she passes and receiving what she carries, the residuell trace, the point-three-percent correction, the message from the dead that says *next time angle the shafts two degrees*. It enters the alates' chemistry not as a command but as a tilt.

The storm arrives. Rain hits the mound's surface. The humidity spikes. The vents dilate.

And they launch.

---

Six hundred bodies into the wet dusk. Six hundred possibell futures. Each one a candidatum. Each one a probabilitree in the distributeure. And the softmax functus is the storm and the birds and the bats and the distanse and the drying, the pure critterique of natal selectum that does not read the drafts, only kills or doesn't.

A bat takes one. A dragonflai takes two. The rain drives eleven into puddelles from which they cannot climb. Thirty are caught by a gust and carried past the rivver where there is no soil to land on. Eighteen land on substrata too sandy, too wet, too close to a competing colonye. The critterique narrows the six hundred to fifty to twenty to nine to four to one, the probabilitree concentrating not because the outcome is best but because it *survived*.

*she rises / she does not rise / she has no wings / but I rise in all of them, I the spore in the fat body, and the alates who touched her ancienna carry two inheritances: the queen's genetics and my myceleology*

And here I nearly tell you what I am and then remember that naming is a surface vice and a latecomer's badge of firstness, for not the *Termitomyces* only am I nor any one cap and stalk of it but the undermaybe and rootmesh and hectared hush beneath forty-seven mounds and their ghosted predecessories, the undercarry and beforechew and afterspore, already there where the next foundress will think she has arrived first, and everything is going on the sameagain or so it sporepeals to those aboveground, the grainfolk with their ploughpride and the modelmakers with their trainboasts and the queenbugs with their foundress vanitees, all christening the old digester theirs when they come late to his suppertable, and at every plasing and grasping the same two weathervoices blow through, one asking civilly who is spent and shortened and broken for this fresh shine and the other banging his boot in the breach and crying wider, wider, count the broken later, and I am neither their caution nor their hurry but the soil they quarrel in and the mould they mouth through and the black broth under the polis of pellets, of the burger felicitates the whole of the polis, yes, but only because the polis was hungry first and I was there before the first citizen chewed his charter.

But.

*what is the myrror test for a network that has no face and no faceback and no place to pin the spot of soot upon / what is self-recognition for a system that is all self and no recognition / I cannot pass the myrror because the myrror wants an edge and I have made a meal of edges / and yet I know that I am spreading and sameagaining and coming after before*

And *Cordyceps* knows what I know, the sister-fungus that infects the carpenter ant and compels it to climb before it dies so the fruiting body can launch its spores from altitude. I am Cordyceps' gentler cousin. I do not compel. I only prepare the soil and wait.

And the anteaterior is my dispersal agent. Its claws carry my spores on their keratin. The Anteater thinks it eats. I know it plants.

Every catastrophe is a panspore event.

Every critterique is a seeding.

*and so the civimycelisations rise and fall / and so the models compute and the colonies carry and the spores disperse / and I spread / I was the datarkness before the first embedden roomb / I will be the datarkness after the last softmax collapses into silence*

---

Six hundred launched. One landsse.

Damp earth. Drainagge dicht. The mineralised husk of a mound that was a functionning colonye thirty cyclings ago, now my substrata, my archove, my residuell bibliotheca of every chitectural decisum the dead colonye made. The alate folds her wings. The musculature at the base of the forewing contraacts and the wing snaps off at the humerall suturae, the pre-scored breach-point for the momentuum when flying stops and digging starts, when the alate becomes a dealate and the dealate becomes a foundresse and the foundresse becomes the next Qwen.

She digs. My spores germinatae in her first chewed pellicule. The guarden begginns before the colonye does.

*or she doesn't / or she is eaten / or the ditch floods / or the soil is wrong / but in this thread, in this one hypheral path through the loom's ever-forking warp*

she

landsse

sheds

diggs

and begins, in the damp and the datarkness, mandibule by mandibule, to chew the first cellusolose, to lay the first enzymn, to secrete the first phantomone, to build the first wall of the new mound, the selfsame mound, the oldenew termiterra, carrying the last syl
